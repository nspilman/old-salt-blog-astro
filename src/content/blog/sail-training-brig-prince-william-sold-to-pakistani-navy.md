![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/09/prince-william.jpg)The [brig](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_William_\(ship\)) *[Prince William](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_William_\(ship\))* [](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_William_\(ship\))owned by the [Tall Ships Youth Trust](http://www.tallships.org/) has been sold to the Pakistan Navy and has been renamed the PNS *Rah Naward*. The *Prince William* was delivered in 2001, a sistership to the [*TS Stavros  S. Niarchos*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stavros_S_Niarchos_\(ship\)).   The *Prince William* was laid up in 2007 and has been actively for sale.  The sale was announced by a the Tall Ships Youth Trust in letter dated September 22 but [posted on their website this morning](http://www.tallships.org/attachments/43/10.09.22%20News%20Release%20incl%20Covering%20Letter.pdf).  In addition to the *Stavros  S. Niarchos,* the Trust also operates four identical 22 m Challenger yachts and a 19 metre catamaran.
