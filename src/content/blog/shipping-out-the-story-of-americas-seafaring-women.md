![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/188138_211110038904244_6796866_n.jpg)For those in New York there is a special program, [Women At Sea: Screening, Conversation, Reception](http://workingharbor.com/index.html), next Wednesday, March 30th, at the [Community Church of New York](http://www.ccny.org/), 40 East 35th Street, New York, NY at 6:00 PM.    The program features a screening of the PBS documentary “[Shipping Out—The Story of America’s Seafaring Women](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gzFDfgTx1_o)” followed by a conversation with seven women mariners who work as captains, mates, engineers and pilots.

Tickets are $20 and can be purchased at through the [Working Harbor Committee](http://www.nycharities.org/events/EventLevels.aspx?ETID=3181).
