It is now official. Watching the [Discovery Channel](http://dsc.discovery.com/) makes you stupid, or at the very least, can leave you less informed than when you started watching.  A poll shows that more than 70% of those who watched the Discovery Channel’s fake “documentary,”  *Megalodon: The Monster Shark Lives*, believe that megalodon is still swimming in the world’s oceans, despite having gone extinct two million years ago.
