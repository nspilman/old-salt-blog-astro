Barque Picton Castle

The barque [*Picton Castle*](http://www.picton-castle.com/), known for its around the world voyages, is offering cruises this summer [sailing around Newfoundland.](http://www.picton-castle.com/voyages/the-voyages/summer-2011.html) It sounds fantastic.

[Sail Around Newfoundland](http://www.picton-castle.com/voyages/the-voyages/summer-2011.html)

*Starkly stunning coastline. Incredible whales, seabirds and maybe even icebergs. Hospitable folks in big towns and small outport villages. And the feeling of having earned it all, working your way there as part of the crew.*  
  
*Step aboard the Picton Castle this summer to sail around the island of Newfoundland. The most eastern piece of land in North America, Newfoundland is known for its breathtaking scenery, maritime heritage, vibrant arts and culture and, perhaps most importantly, for its friendly and welcoming people. Discover all of these things for yourself when you join the Picton Castle as a crew member.*
