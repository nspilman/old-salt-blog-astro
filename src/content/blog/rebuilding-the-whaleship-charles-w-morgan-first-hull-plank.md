The *[Charles W. Morgan](http://www.mysticseaport.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=home.viewpage&page_id=B3E63C64-B3CA-45AE-A83D72C303A9C6BF)* was launched in 1841. She is America’s last surviving wooden whaleship and is the “crown jewel” of the [Mystic Seaport Museum](http://www.mysticseaport.org/) collection.  She has been undergoing restoration at the [Henry B. DuPont Preservation Shipyard](http://www.mysticseaport.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=home.viewPage&page_id=05F74191-D78C-D0C6-64E2AB6C7E72F3B6) at Mystic Seaport since 2010.  Earlier this week, they installed the first new hull plank, a significant milestone. Museum President, Steve White, drove in the first spike.
