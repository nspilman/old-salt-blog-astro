The *[Costa Concordia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Costa_Concordia)* has begun her final voyage from the Tuscan island of Giglio to the scrapyard in Genoa, Italy, the port city where the ill-fated cruise ship was built.  The [ship sank and partially capsized in January 2012](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Costa_Concordia_disaster) after striking a reef. Thirty two passengers and crew died. [Read more here](http://www.cnn.com/2014/07/23/world/europe/costa-concordia-salvage/index.html). Thanks to David for contributing to this post.
