“[A Whale](http://www.marinetraffic.com/ais/shipdetails.aspx?MMSI=636014465)” is by far the biggest oil skimmer ever built.   The bigger question question may be, “Will it work?”  The converted oil/bulk ore carrier is currently docked in Boothville, La., docks as it is made ready for [48 hours of testing in the Gulf](http://www.csmonitor.com/USA/2010/0702/A-Whale-to-the-rescue-Can-super-skimmer-turn-tide-of-Gulf-oil-spill) over the weekend.
