The [Volvo Ocean Race](http://www.volvooceanrace.com/en/home.html) is off on the [third leg from the Maldives to Sanya, China.](http://www.thenational.ae/sport/other-sport/a-strange-start-in-the-maldives-for-volvo-fleet-heading-to-sanya)  The Volvo fleet was again transported from Abu Dhabi by ship to avoid potential attacks by pirates.  During the fleet stop over in Abu Dhabi, a number of the Volvo sailors were invited to sail on the traditional sailing dhows in the Abu Dhabi Sailing Festival Race.
