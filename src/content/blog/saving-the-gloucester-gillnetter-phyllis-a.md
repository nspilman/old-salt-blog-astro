The [gillnetter *Phyllis A*.](http://goodmorninggloucester.wordpress.com/2013/10/21/gillnetter-phyllis-a-circa-1940/), built in 1925, is the oldest fishing vessel in Gloucester, Massachusetts, which calls itself, America’s oldest seaport.  The [Phyllis A. Marine Association, Inc](http://www.phyllis-a.org/home.html).is [raising money](https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/save-our-historic-boat#home) to promote the history of gillnetting in Gloucester, Massachusetts, through the display and preservation of the historic fishing vessel.  All donations are tax-deductible.

[Save Our Historic Boat!](https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/save-our-historic-boat#home)
