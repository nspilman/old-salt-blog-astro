Just in case you think you had a bad Monday, yesterday the [USC container ship *Al Rawdah*](http://www.vesseltracker.com/en/Ships/Al-Rawdah-9349564.html) ran aground on a reef in the waters of Pulau Sambu Stone Chain, Rear Padang, Batam, not long after leaving Port Klang, Selangor, Malaysia.   The ship was bound for China.
