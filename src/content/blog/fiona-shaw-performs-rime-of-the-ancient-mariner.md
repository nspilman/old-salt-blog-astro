[Fiona Shaw](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0789716/), in an excerpt from a new staged reading of Samuel Taylor Coleridge’s 1798 poem “[The Rime of the Ancient Mariner.](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Rime_of_the_Ancient_Mariner)” This scene features the passage that birthed the expression “albatross around your neck.” The show, directed by Phyllida Lloyd, continues through Dec. 22 at the Harvey Theater of the [Brooklyn Academy of Music](http://www.bam.org/).
