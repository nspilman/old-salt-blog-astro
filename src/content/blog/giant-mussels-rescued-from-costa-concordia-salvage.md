An unexpected rescue in the salvage of the Costa Concordia. Scientists discovered rare [pinna nobilis](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pinna_nobilis) mussels at the wreck site and are relocating them so that they are not destroyed by the salvage work. Thanks to Phil Leon for pointing it out.
