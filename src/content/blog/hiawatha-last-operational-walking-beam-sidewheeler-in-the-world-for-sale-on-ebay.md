![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/07/hiawatha.jpg)The *[Hiawatha](http://cgi.ebay.com/ebaymotors/Last-operational-Walking-Beam-Sidewheeler-world-/270611561440?cmd=ViewItem&pt=Other_Boats&hash=item3f01b4bbe0#ht_500wt_1182)*, reportedly the last operational walking beam steam sidewheeler in the world, is being offered for [sale on E-Bay.](http://cgi.ebay.com/ebaymotors/Last-operational-Walking-Beam-Sidewheeler-world-/270611561440?cmd=ViewItem&pt=Other_Boats&hash=item3f01b4bbe0#v4-37) Built in 1983 of steel, the *Hiawatha* is 38 feet long with a 15.5 feet beam.  Its paddle wheels are 7 feet in diameter and the ship has a top speed of 7 knots and cruising speed of 5 knots.  She is currently in Bay City, Michigan.

[Historic Bay City steamboat Hiawatha up for sale on eBay](http://www.mlive.com/news/bay-city/index.ssf/2010/07/historic_bay_city_steamboat_up.html)
