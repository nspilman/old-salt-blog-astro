![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/cowd1.jpg)This year’s [City of Water Day](http://waterfrontalliance.org/what-we-do/city-of-water-day/) hosted, by the [Waterfront Alliance](http://waterfrontalliance.org/), will take place Saturday, July 15th, from 10 AM to 4 PM, on Governors Island in New York harbor as well as at neighborhood sites across the harbor. There will be [free boat rides,](http://waterfrontalliance.org/what-we-do/city-of-water-day/activities/#boat-tours) lots of [children’s activities sponsored by Disney](http://waterfrontalliance.org/what-we-do/city-of-water-day/activities/#childrens-activities) and the ever-popular [Con Edison Cardboard Kayak Race](http://waterfrontalliance.org/what-we-do/city-of-water-day/activities/#cardboard-kayak-race).
