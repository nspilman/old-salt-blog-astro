One year ago today, the ultra-large container ship *[Ever Given](https://www.marinetraffic.com/en/ais/details/ships/shipid:5630138/mmsi:353136000/imo:9811000/vessel:EVER_GIVEN)* [ran aground](https://www.oldsaltblog.com/2021/03/ultra-large-container-ship-ever-given-blocks-suez-canal/), blocking the Suez Canal for six days. The grounding resulted in a traffic jam of roughly 400 ships and cost an estimated $10 billion per day in lost commerce. Here is a short documentary about how and why the accident happened and what was done to free the ship and reopen the canal.
