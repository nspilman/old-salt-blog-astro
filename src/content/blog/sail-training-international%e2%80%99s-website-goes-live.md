[![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/04/Sails.jpg)](http://www.sailtraininginternational.org/home)The new [Sail Training International website](http://www.sailtraininginternational.org/home) has gone live and it is quite impressive. Lots of great information, news and photos. Definitely worth a look.   [Sail Training International](http://www.sailtraininginternational.org/about-us) is a not-for-profit organisation with worldwide membership and activities whose purpose is the development and education of young people through the sail training experience, regardless of nationality, culture, religion, gender or social background.
