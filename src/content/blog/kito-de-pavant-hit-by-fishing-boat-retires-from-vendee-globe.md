A collision with a fishing boat has taken [Kito de Pavant](http://www.vendeeglobe.org/en/skipper/2/kito-de-pavant.html), sailing for Groupe Bel, out of the [Vendée Globe](http://www.vendeeglobe.org/en/) non-stop round-the-world single-handed yacht race, two days into the race and only a  day after [Marc Guillemot](http://www.vendeeglobe.org/en/skipper/9/marc-guillemot.html) was forced to drop out a keel failure on his boat,*[Safran](http://www.vendeeglobe.org/en/skipper/9/marc-guillemot.html#boat).* The collision damaged the outrigger, bowsprit, deck and hull of his boat, though fortunately, de Pavant was not injured.
