If you are looking for something to do this weekend, there is no shortage of maritime festivals.   In addition to [Delaware ‘Bay Day,’](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/06/10/19th-annual-delaware-bay-day-june-11th/) which we posted about earlier today, the following cities and ports are having festivals this weekend:

[The Mackinaw Maritime Festival in Mackinaw City, Michigan](http://www.thegraphicweekly.com/2011/jun/event060211-maritime.html)

[10th Annual Cape Cod Maritime Festival at the Cape Cod Maritime Museum in Hyannis MA](http://www.capecodmaritimemuseum.org/events.html)

[Lake Champlain Maritime Festival on the Lake Champlain Waterfront, Burlington, Vermont](http://www.lcmfestival.com/event-information/)

[1st Annual Taste of Juneau Food and Music Festival](http://www.atasteofjuneau.com/) (not strictly maritime, but pretty close)

[Riverdale RiverFest at the College of Mount St. Vincent in Riverdale, NY](http://www.riverdaleriverfest.org/waterfront.html)
