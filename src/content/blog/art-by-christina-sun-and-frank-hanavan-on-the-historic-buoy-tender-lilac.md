Frank Hanavan’s W.O. Decker

The bulkheads of the [historic buoy tender *Lilac*](http://lilacpreservationproject.org/hoursevents.html) will be graced by the maritime paintings of [Christina Sun](http://bowsprite.wordpress.com/) and [Frank Hanavan](http://www.frankhanavan.com/) from Tuesday, August 7 through August 31.  The *Lilac* is berthed at [Hudson River Park’s Pier 25](http://www.hudsonriverpark.org/explore-the-park/locations/pier-25) at West Street and N. Moore Street in lower Manhattan.
