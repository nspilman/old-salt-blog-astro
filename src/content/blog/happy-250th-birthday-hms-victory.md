In honor of the 250th anniversary of the launching of [HMS *Victory*](http://www.hms-victory.com/), here is a rolling  broadside, fired by none other than HMS *Victory*.  The [ship is beginning a £50million restoration](http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2895451/The-battle-save-HMS-Victory-50million-project-launched-try-preserve-Nelson-s-flagship-nation.html) project in an attempt to preserve Nelson’s flagship for future generations.  For an excellent look at the ship’s history check out Julian Stockwin’s  [VICTORY TURNS 250](http://www.mcbooks.com/pdf/newsletter_b6ceb8c3b871c6eb265fdfc3874c0092.pdf) in the May/June issue of [Quarterdeck](http://www.mcbooks.com/pdf/newsletter_b6ceb8c3b871c6eb265fdfc3874c0092.pdf) by [McBooks Press](http://www.mcbooks.com/).
