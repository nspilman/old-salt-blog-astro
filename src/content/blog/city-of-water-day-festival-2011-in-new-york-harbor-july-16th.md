![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Web-Images-small.jpg)Anyone who needs to be reminded that New York City is indeed a city of water should find their way down to the harbor’s edge on Saturday. The [Metropolitan Waterfront Alliance](http://www.waterfrontalliance.org/) is hosting the [2011 the City of Water Day Festival](http://www.cityofwaterday.org/) in New York Harbor this Saturday, July 16th.  Activities include boat tours, sailing, kayaking, biking, arts, crafts, games, delicious food, and live music at locations along the harbor from the Bronx to Staten Island. These include Governors Island, NY, Liberty State Park, NJ as well as at Bronx River Park, Brooklyn Bridge Park, Harlem River Park, Hudson River Park, Staten Island South Beach, and Yonkers Pier 40.
