Here is a short video of [Pip Hare](https://www.piphare.com/) giving a tour under sail of her foiling [IMOCA 60 *Medallia*](https://www.yachtingmonthly.com/news/medallia-pip-hares-2024-vendee-globe-imoca-60-81514) to Yachting Monthly’s Katy Stickland. Hare recently completed the [Vendée Arctique](https://www.vendeearctique.org/en), a qualifying race for the Vendée Globe 2024/25 in *Medallia.* Pip Hare was the first British skipper to finish the Vendée Globe 2020/21, placing 19th overall. She is also only the 8th woman to ever finish the race.
