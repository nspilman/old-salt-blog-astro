Barque Europa

[Tall Ships Duluth 2010](http://www.visitduluth.com/tall-ships/) has an impressive line up of ships scheduled for their tall ships festival.  The ships are expected to start arriving in Duluth today.  The [Brig](http://flagshipniagara.org/flagship_niagara/index.htm) *[Niagara](http://flagshipniagara.org/flagship_niagara/index.htm)*, the [Barque](http://www.barkeuropa.com/en/home/1-Tall_ship_barque_europa) *[Europa](http://www.barkeuropa.com/en/home/1-Tall_ship_barque_europa)*, [HMS](http://www.tallshipbounty.org/) *[Bounty](http://www.tallshipbounty.org/)*, the *[Pride of Baltimore II](http://www.pride2.org/index.php)*, the *[Roald Amundsen](http://www.sailtraining.de/index.php?id=28&L=1)*, the [schooner](http://www.schoonerman.com/zeeto.htm) *[Zeeto](http://www.schoonerman.com/zeeto.htm)*, the [S/V](http://www.discoveryworld.org/denisSullivan.php) *[Denis Sullivan](http://www.discoveryworld.org/denisSullivan.php)*, and the [schooner](http://www.worldoceanschool.org/) *[Roseway](http://www.worldoceanschool.org/)* [](http://www.worldoceanschool.org/)will sail in a “Grand Parade of Ships” on Thursday afternoon with the tall ships festival getting into full swing Friday through Sunday.

[Tall Ships Duluth 2010 July 28 – August 3](http://www.visitduluth.com/tall-ships/)
