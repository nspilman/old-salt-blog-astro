We recently posted a video about [climbing the rig on the Götheborg of Sweden](https://www.oldsaltblog.com/2022/04/gotheborg-of-sweden-climbing-the-rig/). Here is an interesting application of “[high ropes course](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ropes_course)” techniques and technology to allow the public to safely [climb to the maintop and out on a yard](https://www.rmg.co.uk/cutty-sark/attractions/cutty-sark-rig-climb-experience) on the historic clipper ship [*Cutty Sark*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cutty_Sark), in drydock in Greenwich, UK. And, as is often typical in ropes courses, participants come down by zip line. [Click here to learn more.](https://www.rmg.co.uk/cutty-sark/attractions/cutty-sark-rig-climb-experience)
