A short and lovely video from two years ago about sailing from Milwaukee on the three-masted, wooden schooner [*Denis Sullivan*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denis_Sullivan_\(schooner\)). Like so many other vessels, the schooner’s [2020 sailing season was canceled](https://www.discoveryworld.org/things-to-do/exhibits/sailing-vessel-denis-sullivan/) due to the pandemic and sadly, her [future is uncertain](https://www.cbs58.com/news/lakefront-staple-s-v-denis-sullivan-may-never-sail-again).
