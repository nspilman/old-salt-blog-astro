On [Trafalgar Day](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trafalgar_Day), the anniversary of [Admiral Horatio Nelson](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horatio_Nelson,_1st_Viscount_Nelson)‘s victory and death at the [Battle of Trafalgar](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Trafalgar), a ballad describing the battle, “On board a Man o’ War,” sung by Ian Page. Feel free to grab a mug of porter, or coffee or tea, and to sing along.
