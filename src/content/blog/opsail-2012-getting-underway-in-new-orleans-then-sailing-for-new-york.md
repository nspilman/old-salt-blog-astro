![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2012/04/opsail2012-logo-home.jpg)The 47th Operation Sail, [OpSail 2012](http://www.opsail.org/extra.php) gets underway tomorrow, [April 17th and will continue through the 23rd](http://nolanavyweek.com/schedule-of-events-april-17-23-2012.html), in the port of New Orleans, LA.   It will feature the tall ships *[Dewaruci](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KRI_Dewaruci)* from Indonesia, the *[BAE Guayas](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BAE_Guayas_\(BE-21\))* from Equador and the *[USCG Eagle](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USCG_Eagle)* from the United States.  Six non-sail naval vessels from four nations will also be in participation.

From May 23-30, 2012, OpSail will shift to New York harbor. The [Dewaruci](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KRI_Dewaruci),  *[BAE Guayas](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BAE_Guayas_\(BE-21\))* and the *[USCG Eagle](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USCG_Eagle)* will be joined by the Class A & B tall ships*,  [Cisne Branco](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cisne_Branco)* from Brazil, *[Gloria](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ARC_Gloria)* from Colombia, *[Juan Sebastian De El Cano](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_ship_Juan_Sebasti%C3%A1n_Elcano_\(1927\))* from Spain, *[Cuauhtemoc](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ARM_Cuauht%C3%A9moc_\(BE01\))* from Mexico, and *[Etoile](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%89toile_\(A_649\))* and *[Belle Poule](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belle_Poule_\(A_650\))* from France.  other sailing vessels participating include the *Adirondack*, *America 2.0*, *Clipper City, Gazela Primeiro, Pioneer, Pride of Baltimore II, Shearwater* and the *Summerwind.*

After New York, OpSail 2012 will sail to Norfolk (June 1-12), Baltimore (June 13-19), Boston (June 30-July 5) and New London (July 6-8.)  Thanks to Irwin Irwin Bryan and  Miroslav at [Antic.org](http://antic.org/) for contributing to the post.
