In a dense fog, at about 9:00 AM on May 20, 1899, the 270-foot steamer [*Florida*](http://www.superiortrips.com/Huron/Florida_History.htm) was nearly cut in half by the *George W. Roby*. The ship is sitting upright approximately 200 feet below the surface of Lake Huron, off Presque Isle, Michigan.  The video below, produced by [Michael C. Barnette](http://www.youtube.com/user/aocfishman00) gives us a look at the [remarkably well preserved wreck.](http://www.wrecksite.eu/wreck.aspx?142701)
