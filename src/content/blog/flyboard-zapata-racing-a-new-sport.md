In December of 2011, we posted about the [Zapata Water-Jet Flyboard](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/12/instead-of-a-jet-pack-the-zapata-water-jet-flyboard/), a sort of personal jetpack connected to a powerful jet ski. Now, just over a year later, we learn that the [Flyboard World Cup competition](http://www.zapata-racing.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/flyboard-doha-com-gb.pdf) took place in Doha, Qatar on the third weekend of October attracting 50 competitors from 17 countries. it appears that a new fringe sport may be developing.
