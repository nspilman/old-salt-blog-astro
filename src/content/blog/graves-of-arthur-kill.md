Just a few miles from the gleaming glass towers of lower Manhattan is the old Witte yard, a graveyard of ships, a little known, slightly surreal assemblage of ghostly remains along the waterway known as Arthur Kill.  [Gary Kane](http://www.3fishproductions.com/) and [Will Van Dorp](http://tugster.wordpress.com/) have produced a documentary about this strange and special place.
