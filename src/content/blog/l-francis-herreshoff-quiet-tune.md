[L. Francis Herreshoff](http://www.herreshoffcastle.com/LFrancis.html) was the son of naval architect, Nathanael Greene Herreshoff and the father of yacht designer and naval architect Halsey Herreshoff. L. Francis was widely respected as was a boat designer, naval architect, editor and author of books and magazine articles. Here, Doug Hylan speaks of L. Francis Herreshoff and QUIET TUNE, one of his designs.
