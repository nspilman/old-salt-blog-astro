![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/tallinnpano.jpg)We are several months late in posting about [Tallinn Maritime Days](http://www.portoftallinn.com/news?art=876) from last July. The port of Tallinn, on the Gulf of Finland, is the capital and largest city of Estonia and hosted 50 sailing ships in this summer’s Tallships Race. Here are some amazing 3D VR images of the festival.  [Click here for a panoramic view](http://www.360pano.eu/show/?id=400) of the Norwegian full-rigged ship, [*Christian Radich*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_Radich). [Click here for another panoramic view](https://www.360cities.net/image/tallinn-maritime-days-ahoy-1) of the festival shot by Andrew Bodrov. Click and drag with your mouse to see the full images.
