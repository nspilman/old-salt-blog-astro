Happy Thanksgiving! Today has been celebrated as a day of Thanksgiving in the United States on the third Thursday of November since 1863. The holiday is notionally based on a harvest feast in 1621 between Native Americans and Puritans who had arrived on the ship [*Mayflower*.](http://mayflowerhistory.com/history-of-the-mayflower/)  In honor of the day, here is a short video of the replica [*Mayflower II*](https://www.plimoth.org/what-see-do/mayflower-ii)   arriving in New York in 1957. The *Mayflower II* is currently undergoing a [multi-year restoration](https://www.mysticseaport.org/category/mayflower-ii-restoration/) in the Henry B. duPont Preservation Shipyard at the Mystic Seaport Museum.
