Seventy-five years today, the Royal Navy sank the [German battleship *Bismark*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/German_battleship_Bismarck) in a three-day running battle in which the Bismark sunk the British battle cruiser HMS *Hood*.  Ultimately, the German battleship was disabled when her rudder was damaged in a torpedo attack by obsolescent [Fairey Swordfish biplane torpedo bombers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fairey_Swordfish) from the aircraft carrier HMS *Ark Royal.*   Here is a British newsreel of the events.  Thanks to [Alaric Bond](https://www.alaricbond.com/) for contributing to this post.
