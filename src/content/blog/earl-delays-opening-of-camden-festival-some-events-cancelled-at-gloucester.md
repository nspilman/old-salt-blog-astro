Hurricane Earl appears not to be likely to make landfall on the US East Coast but the expected winds and seas have rearranged some schedules. The [Camden Windjammer Festival](http://www.onlinesentinel.com/news/Camden-Windjammer-Festival-changes-schedule-due-to-hurricane.html) originally scheduled to start today has been postponed to 4PM on Saturday.   The [Gloucester Schooner Festival](http://www.capeannvacations.com/schooner/schedule/) has cancelled several outdoor activities but otherwise is on course, for now.
