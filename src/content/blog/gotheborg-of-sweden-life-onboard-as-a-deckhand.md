A short video that seeks to answer the question, what is it like to sail onboard [Götheborg of Sweden](https://www.gotheborg.se/) as a deckhand? Have a look at what happens onboard: From bringing your passport when you sign on, to rig training, lessons, fire round, lookout post – and all the other fun things that happen onboard!

This short video was shot in September last year (2021) when the organization’s Head of Communication sailed with the ship from Stockholm to Gothenburg, and “volunteered” to be the guinea pig for explaining life on board.
