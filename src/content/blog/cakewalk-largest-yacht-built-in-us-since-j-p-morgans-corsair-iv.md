While we may prefer prefer vessels with more sticks, cloth and string, we are overdue in acknowledging the construction of  *[Cakewalk](http://www.charterworld.com/news/derecktor-launch-85-motor-yacht-cakewalk-largest-usa-history)*, a new  $82 million 85 meter yacht, which was delivered from [Derecktor Shipyards](http://www.derecktor.com/) in Bridgeport, CT yard in August. *Cakewalk* is the largest yacht built in the US since Bath Iron Works built the Corsair IV for J.P. Morgan in 1930.
