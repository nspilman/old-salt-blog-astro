While here in New York harbor, we were cheering on tug boats racing along belching black smoke, the good folks in [Gloucester, Massachusetts were celebrating classic schooners](http://capeannvacations.com/schooner/).  The tug boat race was great fun, but I do so love schooners and the schooners at Gloucester put on quite a show.
