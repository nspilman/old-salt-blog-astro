In an upcoming PBS documentary, [*Octopus: Making Contact*](https://www.pbs.org/wnet/nature/octopus-making-contact-preview-a98u9n/19851/), a scientist observes an octopus changing color while sleeping upside down in a tank. Is it dreaming? Marine biologist Dr. David Scheel speculates what the dream might be in accordance with the particular shade of camouflage being exhibited at each moment.
