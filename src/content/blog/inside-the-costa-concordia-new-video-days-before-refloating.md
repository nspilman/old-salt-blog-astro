In just over a week, salvors expect to  start pumping air into sponson tanks welded along the hull of the [*Costa Concordia*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Costa_Concordia) [to refloat the ship](http://www.cbc.ca/news/world/costa-concordia-refloating-to-be-complete-in-10-days-1.2695635).   Prior to the refloating, Italian police have released footage showing the underwater interior of the wrecked cruise ship.
