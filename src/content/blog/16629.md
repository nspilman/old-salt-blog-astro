![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2011/02/2queens1.jpg)On January 14th we posted about the [Royal Rendezvous in New York harbor](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/01/14/cunard-takes-manhattan-the-three-queens-rendezvous-in-the-harbor/), the rendezvous of the three Queens of the Cunard Line, the [Queen Mary 2](http://www.cunard.com/Ships/Queen-Mary-2/)*,  the* *[Queen Victoria](http://www.cunard.com/Ships/Queen-Victoria/),* and the [Queen Elizabeth](http://www.cunard.com/Ships/Queen-Elizabeth/).  Now just over a month later the *Queen Mary 2* and *Queen Elizabeth* entered Sydney harbor together at 6am local time, Tuesday.

[Two queens of the sea arrive in Sydney](http://news.ninemsn.com.au/national/8214454/superships-arrive-in-sydney-harbour)
