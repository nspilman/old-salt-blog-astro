A short video of [Tradewind Voyages’ *Golden Horizon*](https://www.tradewindvoyages.com/our-ship/) sailing at night between Poole & Portland. *Golden Horizon i*s the largest square-rigged sailing ship in the world. A 272 passenger, five-masted barque, she is 532 feet (162 meters) long, with a 60 foot (18.5 meter) beam and will have a sail area of 68,300 square feet (6,347 square meters). By comparison, the tea clipper *[Cutty Sark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cutty_Sark)* set less than half as much sail at around 3,000 square meters. The ship was inspired by the [*France II*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France_II) of 1912.
