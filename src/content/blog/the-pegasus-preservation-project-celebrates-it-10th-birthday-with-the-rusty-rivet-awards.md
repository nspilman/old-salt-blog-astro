The tug [*Pegasus*](http://www.tugpegasus.org/) was built in 1907.  Her last major refit was in 1953 when her steam engine was removed and a diesel engine installed.   Tonight the [Pegasus Preservation Project](http://www.tugpegasus.org/index.htm) is celebrating the 10th year of restoration of the Tug *Pegasus* with the First Annual Rusty Rivet Awards to “eight treasured volunteers.”  The festivities are taking place this evening  from 5:30 PM-7:30 PM at the Woolworth Tower Kitchen, 9 Barclay Street (at Broadway), New York, NY.
