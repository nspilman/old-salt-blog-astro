[![caribbeanfantasy](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/caribbeanfantasy.jpg)A fire broke out on the passenger/roro ferry](http://www.nbcnews.com/news/world/more-500-evacuated-cruise-ship-fire-near-puerto-rico-n632651) *[Caribbean Fantasy](https://www.vesselfinder.com/vessels/CARIBBEAN-FANTASY-IMO-8814263-MMSI-372067000)* as she was approaching San Juan, Puerto Rico on Wednesday morning. A reported 512 passengers were evacuated from the burning ship, roughly a mile off Puerto Rico’s north coast. The fire was said to have started in the engine room and to have spread. Thus far only four injuries to passengers and crew have been reported and no one has been reported to have died or to be missing.

*[Caribbean Fantasy](https://www.vesselfinder.com/vessels/CARIBBEAN-FANTASY-IMO-8814263-MMSI-372067000)* was built in 1989 and is registered in Panama. The passenger/roro ferry is 176m long with a 27m beam and is 27,362 gross tonnes.
