![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/u1_deneb01.jpg)Another capsize in the news. Last Saturday, the container feeder ship [*Deneb*](http://www.shipspotting.com/gallery/photo.php?lid=698837) capsized at the Maersk terminal in Algeciras, Spain.   Cargo handling had been shut down  due to stability concerns. A few hours later the ship rapidly listed to 50 degrees.    Two crew members, including the captain were slightly injured.  A [Svitzer](http://www.svitzer.com/) salvage vessel is now unloading the capsized container ship.
