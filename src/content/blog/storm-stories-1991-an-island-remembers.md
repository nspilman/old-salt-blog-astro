![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/nsl_logo_main.jpg)What looks like a  great exhibition, “[Storm Stories! 1991: An Island Remembers](http://www.nantucketshipwreck.org/changing-exhibitions)” opened yesterday at the [Nantucket Shipwreck & Lifesaving Museum](http://www.nantucketshipwreck.org/nantucketshipwreck) and will run through October 10, 2011.   The exhibition commemorats the 20th anniversaries of Hurricane Bob and The No-Name Storm.

[The Egan Maritime Institute announces “Storm Stories! 1991: An Island Remembers” May 27- October 10, 2011 at the Nantucket Shipwreck & Lifesaving Museum](http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/the-egan-maritime-institute-announces-storm-stories-1991-an-island-remembers-may-27--october-10-2011-at-the-nantucket-shipwreck--lifesaving-museum-121154459.html)
