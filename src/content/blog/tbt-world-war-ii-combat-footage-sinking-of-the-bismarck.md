On Throw-Back-Thursday, here is footage of the sinking of the German battleship [*Bismark*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/German_battleship_Bismarck), 79 years ago yesterday, by the Royal Navy in a three-day running battle in which the *Bismark* sank the British battlecruiser [HMS *Hood*.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Hood)  Ultimately, the German battleship was disabled when her rudder was jammed in a torpedo attack by obsolescent [Fairey Swordfish biplane torpedo bombers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fairey_Swordfish) from the aircraft carrier [HMS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Ark_Royal_\(91\)) *Ark Royal.*   Here is a British newsreel of the events.  Thanks to [Alaric Bond](https://www.alaricbond.com/) for contributing to this post.
