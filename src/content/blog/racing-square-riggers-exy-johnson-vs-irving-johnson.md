Last September we posted about [The Great Brigantine Race of 2011](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/09/16/the-great-brigantine-race-of-2001/) off Newport Beach, CA, between two identical 90′ brigantines, the [*Irving Johnson* and the *Exy Johnson*](http://www.brigantines.com/default.html) .  Launched in 2002, they are part of the [Los Angeles Maritime Institute’s “TopSail Youth Program,](http://www.lamitopsail.org/)” a sail training “education and adventure” program.
