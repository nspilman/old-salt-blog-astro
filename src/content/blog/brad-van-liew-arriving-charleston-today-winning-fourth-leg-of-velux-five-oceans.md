Brad Van Liew sailing  *Le Pingouin* is expected to cross the finish line in Charleston, SC, his adopted home town, some time today, continuing to dominate the [Velux Five Oceans](http://www.velux5oceans.com/) singlehanded around the world race.  Thus far, he has won all four legs of the around the world race thus far.   Following Van Liew are  Derek Hatfield, sailing *Active House*, and Chris Stanmore-Major sailing *Spartan*.  Zbigniew ‘Gutek’ Gutkowski sailing *Operon Racing* was forced to put into Forteleza after his boat’s forestay broke. ‘Gutek’ was also suffering from a broken rib.
