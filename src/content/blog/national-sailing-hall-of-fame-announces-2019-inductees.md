![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2019HOF.jpg)The [National Sailing Hall of Fame](https://nshof.org/) (NSHOF) announced 10 individuals in its 2019 class of inductees. It is an eclectic mix. The recipients include Sailmaker [Robbie Doyle](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1284-doyle-robbie-2019-hall-of-fame); Olympic Gold Medalist [Buddy Friedrichs](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1286-friedrichs-buddy-2019-hall-of-fame-2); sailing’s first-ever Women’s Olympic Gold Medalist [Allison Jolly](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1285-jolly-allison-2019-hall-of-fame); passionate sailor [Capt. John Bonds](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1281-bonds-john-2019-hall-of-fame), whose leadership in establishing safety protocols has had a global impact on offshore sailing; and America’s Cup captain and author, [Arthur Knapp](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1287-knapp-arthur-2019-hall-of-fame), Jr. who is being recognized with the NSHOF Lifetime Achievement Award.  

Included in this year’s list is a historic lineup of engineers, designers, and builders, including clipper shipbuilder [Donald McKay](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1288-mckay-donald-2019-hall-of-fame); the grandfather of fiberglass production, [Everett A. Pearson](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1289-pearson-everett-2019-hall-of-fame); and the pioneering yacht designer [Doug Peterson](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1290-pearson-doug-2019-hall-of-fame). 

Also included in this year’s inductees are journalists [Thomas F. Day](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1283-day-thomas-2019-hall-of-fame), editor of *Rudder* and founder, in 1906, of The Newport Bermuda Race; and *Yachting* magazine editor and publisher [Herbert Lawrence Stone.](https://nshof.org/hall-of-fame/class-of-2019/418-the-national-sailing-hall-of-fame/hall-of-fame-2019/1291-stone-herbert-lawrence-2019-hall-of-fame)
