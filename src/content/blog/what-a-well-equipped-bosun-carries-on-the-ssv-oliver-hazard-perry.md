![bosungearohp](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2015/10/bosungearohp.jpg)The bosun’s gear on the [SSV](http://www.ohpri.org/) *[Oliver Hazard Perry](http://www.ohpri.org/).* A nice rig. My only concern would be managing the lanyards. Easy to catch on things while climbing about.

From the [Oliver Hazard Perry blog](http://www.ohpri.org/the-ships-blog/every-day-carry-tall-ship-sailor-aboard-oliver-hazard-perry): *Being a full time sailor aboard any Tall Ship is a difficult job encompassing an incredible amount of knowledge and tasks. Everything from complex rigging knowledge and how to sail a square rig ship, all the way to woodworking and organization. It’s not an easy job and it goes well beyond the normal 9-5, but most sailors wouldn’t trade it for anything. Here are some of the tools carried around on a day to day basis that make sailors lives easier.*
