On July 7, 2010, a [barge struck the disabled “Duck boat” DUKW 34](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/07/07/philadelphia-duck-boat-overturns-in-collision-with-barge-in-delaware-river/) at anchor in the Delaware River off Philadelphia.  Of the 35 passengers aboard, two died.  Last November, the [tug’s mate was sentenced](http://www.duckboatdisaster.com/Newsroom/MediaCoverage/Tugboatpilotgetsyearinjailforaccident.aspx) to a year and a day in prison for his role in the collision.  Today, lawyers representing the two victim’s families in a civil wrongful death lawsuit, released a previously unseen video of the collision.  Thanks to Tony Seideman for passing it along.
