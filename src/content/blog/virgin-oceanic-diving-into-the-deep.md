After dabbling with space ships, [Richard Branson](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Branson) has turned to the deep oceans.   The [Virgin Oceanic](http://www.virginoceanic.com/) team will be diving to the deepest ocean depths in a one man submarine designed by [Graham Hawkes](http://www.deepflight.com/team/graham.htm).  The submarine has an operating depth of 37,000ft (7 miles) and is capable of operating for 24hrs unaided. Its top speed is around 3 knots.
