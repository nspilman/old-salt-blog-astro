[Sultana Education Foundation](http://sultanaeducation.org/)‘s Downrigging Weekend Tall Ship and Wooden Boat Festival has evolved into one of the largest annual Tall Ship gatherings on the East Coast. Here is some lovely drone footage by [Shore Studios](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC_penie2K3blLB3QQ7M2wqw) from the 18th Sultana Downrigging Weekend in Chestertown, MD from the end of October. Next year’s [Downrigging festival is scheduled for November 1-3, 2019.](http://sultanaeducation.org/public-programs/sultana-public-sails/downrigging-weekend-2/)
