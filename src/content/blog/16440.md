Here is a recent watercolor by Hans Breeman showing the *MV Rotte* owned by NV Houtvaart Rotterdam. The vessel is shown in Hongkong on charter to K-Line (Kawasaki Kisen Kaisha Ltd, Tokyo).  Hans Breeman is a Dutch maritime painter who focuses on the merchant ships of the 50s and 60s.  To see more of his marvelous work visit his site  – [Hans Breeman and Maritime Art](http://home.tiscali.nl/hansbreeman/indexuk.htm).
