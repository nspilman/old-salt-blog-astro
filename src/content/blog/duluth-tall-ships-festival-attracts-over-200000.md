The [Duluth Tall Ship’s Festival](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/07/28/tall-ships-duluth-2010-july-28-august-3/) which runs through today, has been a major success, attracting over 200,000 visitors.  One of the biggest “stars” of the event was the *[HMS Bounty](http://www.tallshipbounty.org/).* The wait to get on the *Bounty* averaged between two and four hours on Friday.

Not all the action was taking place on the *Bounty* however.  Two of the 200,000 visitors were [Erin Somers and RJ Johnson, who were married](http://www.twincities.com/minnesota/ci_15659441?nclick_check=1) on the *[Pride of Baltimore II.](http://www.pride2.org/index.php)*
