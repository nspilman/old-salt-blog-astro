![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/johnr.jpg)For anyone in the area, sailor and author [John Rousmaniere](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Rousmaniere) will be a guest at [Mystic Seaport Museum](http://www.mysticseaport.org/) on Thursday, December 9th for a wine and cheese reception beginning at at 5:30 p.m, followed by a presentation of  sea stories beginning around 6:00 p.m.  The program concludes by 7:30 p.m. after questions and answers, book signings, and the drawing of the prize-winning raffle ticket for a free copy of one of his books, the winner’s choice.  
  
Rousmaniere is the author of twenty eight books primarily focussed on sailing and the sea, including *[Fastnet, Force 10](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0393308650?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0393308650);* *[After the Storm : True Stories of Disaster and Recovery at Sea](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0071427929?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0071427929);* *[The Annapolis Book of Seamanship](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0684854201?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0684854201);* *[Sleek: Classic Sailboat Photography from the Rosenfeld Collection at Mystic Seaport](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0939510901?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0939510901); [In A Class By Herself: The Yawl Bolero](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0939511134?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0939511134)*;  *[A](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0939511177?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0939511177) [Berth to Bermuda: One Hundred Years of the World’s Classic Ocean Race](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0939511177?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0939511177);  [Sailing at Fishers](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0939510936?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0939510936)*.     Click [here to learn more](http://www.mysticseaport.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=home.viewPage&page_id=12707BA5-1E4F-379B-601099A7D4BA1118).
