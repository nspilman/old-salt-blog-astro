![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/seaport1.jpg)Following up on the [previous news](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/05/16/a-new-plan-and-new-hopes-for-new-yorks-south-street-seaport-museum/) that the current board of the financially troubled [South Street Seaport Museum](http://www.seany.org/) is being replaced, that the Attorney General has barred the removal of the museum’s historic ships from New York harbor and that new funding is being arranged, the [Save our Seaport](http://saveourseaport.wordpress.com/) grassroots organization is sponsoring  a [“Rally to Save our Seaport”](http://saveourseaport.wordpress.com/2011/05/19/rally-to-save-our-seaport-sunday-may-22-2-pm/) on Sunday, May 22, at 2PM,  on South Street at Pier 16 just below the [Peking](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peking_\(ship\))‘s bowsprit.   It will be a great opportunity for friends, supporters and volunteers to show their enthusiasm for the Seaport and its long term survival and future growth.
