As a brief respite from the current madness, here is a mixed-up montage of sailing footage from the Castle Films newsreel named *Windjammer Thrills*. The newsreel starts with the square-rigged training ship *[Christian Radich](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_Radich)*, moves on to fishing schooner races with a nice shot of the *[Gertrude L.Theobald](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gertrude_L._Thebaud)*, then jumps to the America’s Cup, dinghy sailing, iceboating, and beach sailing. An entertaining hodgepodge.
