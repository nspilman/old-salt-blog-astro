In December of 2012, we [posted about the keel laying](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2012/12/peru-lays-keel-of-la-union-to-be-the-largest-sail-training-ship-in-south-america/) for the Peruvian Navy’s sail training ship at the Marine Industrial Services (SIMA) shipyard in Callao. Last month, the new four-masted barque, [BAP (Buque Armada Peruana) *Unión*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BAP_Uni%C3%B3n_\(BEV-161\)) was commissioned. The ship will set approximately 4,324 m2 of sail and has a complement of 250 officers and trainees. The BAP *Unión* is the largest sail training ship in Latin America.
