Here is a fascinating new approach to the design of a mast and sail. The developers at [Next Technologies](http://inflatedwingsails.com/) claim that their Inflated Wing Sail (IWS) is stable in every wind conditions and offers a smooth balanced new way of sailing. No more winches, halyards, shrouds or complex deck gear.
