Happy Thanksgiving!

On Thanksgiving eve, here is a short video of the voyage of the *[Mayflower II](https://www.plimoth.org/what-see-do/mayflower-ii)* across the Atlantic in 1957, under the command of [Captain Alan Villiers.](https://www.oldsaltblog.com/2014/12/alan-villiers-mayflower-ii-joseph-conrad-and-mystic-seaport/) The reproduction was built in [Devon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Devon "Devon"), [England](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/England "England"), during 1955–1956, in a collaboration between Englishman [Warwick Charlton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Warwick_Charlton "Warwick Charlton") and the [Plimoth Patuxet Museum.](https://plimoth.org/) The work drew upon reconstructed ship blueprints held by the American museum, along with hand construction by English shipbuilders using traditional methods.
