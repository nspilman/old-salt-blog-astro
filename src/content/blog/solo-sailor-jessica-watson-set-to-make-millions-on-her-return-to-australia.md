[Solo sailor Jessica Watson set to make millions when she finishes](http://www.news.com.au/business/solo-sailor-jess-set-to-make-millions/story-e6frfm1i-1225834980029?from=public_rss)  
  
*Jessica Watson could be one of the country’s richest teenagers, with a multi-million-dollar sponsorship bonanza awaiting her return to dry land.   Jessica, 16, who sailed out of Australian waters in a rush of controversy, has entered the home stretch of her journey and will return in late April-early May to a warm welcome and live TV coverage.   There will also be a raft of deals awaiting the Sunshine Coast girl’s signature, which could make her the highest-earning Aussie teen.*
