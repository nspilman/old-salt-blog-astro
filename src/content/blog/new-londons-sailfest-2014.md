[New London’s Sailfest](http://www.sailfestnl.org/) kicked off yesterday and will continue through Sunday, Jul 13th. It is billed as Southeastern Connecticut’s premier summertime event attracting over 300,000 people over the three-day festival. The festival features a major fireworks display, amusement rides, and over 200 vendors lining the downtown streets. Sailfest produces multiple stages of free entertainment with musical genres ranging in Rock, Celtic, Hip Hop, and Blues. Kids enjoy free shows at the City Pier stage. Sailfest also showcases the 4th largest fireworks display in the Northeast, sponsored by the Mashantucket Pequot Tribal Nation.  [It is unclear whether the schooner *Amistad* will be attending.](http://foxct.com/2014/07/11/new-londons-sailfest-2014-set-to-begin-amistad-likely-wont-be-there/)   Saturday – 10:00am-11:00pm & Sunday – 9:00am-6:00pm.
