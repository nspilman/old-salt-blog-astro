Last November, we [posted about a concept design](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2017/11/americas-cup-concept-yacht-ac75-foils-no-keel/) for an America’s Cup racer, a 75′ foiling monohull, without a keel. The concept AC75 was presented as a 3D animation. About a year later, the New York Yacht Club’s half sized 38′ prototype, *American Magic*, AKA the Mule, has flown across Narragansett Bay.
