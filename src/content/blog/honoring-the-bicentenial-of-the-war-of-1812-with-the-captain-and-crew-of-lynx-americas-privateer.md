![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2012/03/schoonerLYNX.jpg)On April 14th, at the [Mystic Seaport Museum](http://www.mysticseaport.org/) there will be a celebration of the anniversary of the War of 1812 with the captain and crew of the [*Lynx*, “America’s Privateer.”](http://www.privateerlynx.com/)   Historian and award winning author, William H. White, will be a special guest.  The event will include tours of the ship, a performance by the [Sailing Masters of 1812 Fife & Drum Corps](http://www.sailingmasters.org/), and a book signing of White’s *“Our Flag Was Still There.*”  There will also be live and silent auctions for a position as guest racer onboard [Team Artemis AC45](http://www.americascup.com/en/Teams/Artemis-Racing/Latest/Gallery/2012/3/Artemis-Racing-test-AC45-Wing-Extensions/) – America’s Cup World Series – Newport, RI,  a 5 night oceanfront cottage at the beautiful Waimea Plantation on the island of Kauai, and a one week Nantucket home getaway.  To learn more about the [Lynx Bicentennial Celebration click here](http://www.mysticseaport.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=home.viewPage&page_id=CF2E71B6-1E4F-379B-60D0F5590E9E27DB). Please RSVP by April 4 by calling the Advancement Department at 860-572-5365 or emailing advancement@mysticseaport.org. Space is limited. Click [here to support the Lynx Educational Foundation and the Intrepid Fallen Heroes Fund.](http://www.privateerlynx.com/donate.html)
