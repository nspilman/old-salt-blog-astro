![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/collier1.jpg)The collier *Shen Neng 1* has been refloated and removed from Australia’s Great Barrier Reef where it [grounded ten days ago](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/04/04/shen-neng-1-slams-into-great-barrier-reef-off-australia/).   In its wake it has left severe damage to the reef.  As reported by the [BBC](http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/8616880.stm):  *The Australian authorities have said a Chinese bulk carrier which ran aground off Queensland has caused widespread damage to the famed Great Barrier Reef. The cleanup is likely to be the biggest operation ever undertaken there.* [Toxic paint](http://www.stuff.co.nz/world/australia/3574416/Toxic-paint-killing-Barrier-Reef-coral) from the ship’s hull is reported to have killed coral in a swath over a kilometer long.  Oil spilled from the ship’s bunker tanks has washed up at a nature sanctuary threatening birds and baby turtles.  The [Chinese owner of the ship has apologized](http://open.salon.com/blog/rick_spilman/2010/04/14/THE%20Chinese%20owner%20of%20the%20carrier%20that%20smashed%20into%20the%20Great%20Barrier%20Reef%20has%20apologised) while the government of Australia has [arrested two of the ship’s senior officers](http://www.stuff.co.nz/world/australia/3583453/Arrests-over-Great-Barrier-Reef-grounding).
