Here is reminder of what the Southern Ocean can be like even in the Summer months. About a month ago the [MS *Veendam*](http://www.hollandamerica.com/cruise-vacation-onboard/Veendam) made a cruise from the Falklands to Patagonia and ran into a storm. Waves of over 30 meters and winds of 70 knots were reported. Fortunately there was no reported damage to the ship or injuries to passengers or crew.
