The six boats competing in the [Volvo Ocean Race](http://www.volvooceanrace.com/en/home.html) departed from Alicante, Spain yesterday and were immediately battered by rough seas and high winds while still in the Mediterranean.  The [Abu Dhabi Ocean Racing](http://www.volvooceanraceabudhabi.com/)‘s *Azzam* lost her mast only six hours and 85 nautical miles from the start of the race.  *[Team Sanya](http://www.teamsanya.com/)* withdrew from the race after reporting hull damage in over 40 knot winds and over 30 foot waves.  The Volvo Ocean Race (formerly the Whitbread Round the World Race) is a yacht race around the world, held every three years.   The first leg is from Alicante, Spain to Capetown, South Africa.  Thanks to Ulrich Rudofsky for passing the story along.
