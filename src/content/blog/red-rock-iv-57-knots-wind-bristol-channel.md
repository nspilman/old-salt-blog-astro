A video for a Saturday. Sailing on [*Red Rock IV*](http://rbsailing.blogspot.com/2014/08/red-rock-iv-frers-two-tonner.html)  in 57 knots of wind in the Bristol Channel on June 28, 2016. *Red Rock IV* is a 43 footer designed by German Frers and built by Marland Marine for owner E Mandelbaum, as part of the Argentinian team to compete in the 1979 Admirals Cup.
