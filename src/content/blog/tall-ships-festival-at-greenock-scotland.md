![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/royalistmm.jpg)If there are tall ships in port, there must be a party going on.  [About 60 ships are expected at Greenock’s James Watt Dock](http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-glasgow-west-14081379) from the first leg of the [Tall Ships 2011](http://www.sailtraininginternational.org/events/2011-onwards/2011-race) race from Waterford. Estimates range from 100,000 – 800,000 visitors expected in Greenock for the four day festival.
