Photos from the Sultana Downrigging Weekend in Chestertown, MD. A touch rainy, cold and windy but still a great time. Along with the schooner Sultana were the pinnance [*Kalmar Nyckel*,](http://www.kalmarnyckel.org/)  and the schooners [*Lady Maryland*](https://livingclassrooms.org/faq_ladymaryland.php), [*Pride of Baltimore II*](http://www.pride2.org/) and *[A.J. Meerwald](http://bayshorecenter.org/a-j-meerwald/).*  A variety of classic smaller craft were also in attendance including the sharpy schooner [*E.E. Moore*](http://www.myeasternshoremd.com/news/kent_county/article_30bf400a-7f39-5f14-9669-adb91d939ef2.html) and the vintage catboat *[Silent Maid](http://www.cruisingworld.com/sailboats/silent-maid-loud-statement).*
