![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/05/amistadlogo.jpg)After suffering what was described as [“serious rigging failure”](http://www.facebook.com/SchoonerAmistad/posts/109570512415017) in heavy seas off the Florida coast last week, the [schooner](http://www.facebook.com/SchoonerAmistad) *[Amistad](http://www.facebook.com/SchoonerAmistad)* has returned to Mystic, CT for repairs.   She is expected to “be there for some time.”  The *Amistad* was built at [Mystic Seaport](http://www.mysticseaport.org/) between 1998 and 2000.   The *Amistad* was scheduled to participate in the [2010 Great Lakes United Tall Ships Challenge](http://www.sailtraining.org/tallships/2010greatlakes/) but has withdrawn due to the extent of the repairs required.     The *Amistad* will be replaced in the event by the [Dutch barque](http://translate.google.com/translate?hl=en&sl=nl&u=http://www.barkeuropa.com/&ei=mtz-S-nwMsGB8gbDhIXPDQ&sa=X&oi=translate&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CBsQ7gEwAA&prev=/search%3Fq%3DDutch%2Bbarque%2BEuropa%26hl%3Den%26safe%3Doff) *[Europa](http://translate.google.com/translate?hl=en&sl=nl&u=http://www.barkeuropa.com/&ei=mtz-S-nwMsGB8gbDhIXPDQ&sa=X&oi=translate&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CBsQ7gEwAA&prev=/search%3Fq%3DDutch%2Bbarque%2BEuropa%26hl%3Den%26safe%3Doff).*
