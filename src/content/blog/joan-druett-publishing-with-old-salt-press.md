[![Joan-210](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Joan-210.jpg)Joan Druett](http://www.joan.druett.gen.nz/), the award winning maritime historian and novelist, will be publishing her *Promise of Gold* trilogy with [Old Salt Press](http://oldsaltpress.com/).  The three novels; *[Judas Island](http://oldsaltpress.com/judas-island-by-joan-druett/)*, *[Calafia’s Kingdom](http://oldsaltpress.com/calafias-kingdom/)*, and *[Dearest Enemy](http://oldsaltpress.com/dearest-enemy/);* will be be published as e-books on Amazon in the next few days.   Joan’s *[A Love of Adventure](http://oldsaltpress.com/joan-druetts-love-of-adventure/) and* [*The Beckoning Ice*](http://oldsaltpress.com/joan-druetts-the-beckoning-ice/) – the fifth in her Wiki Coffin nautical mystery series, have been re-released under the Old Salt imprint. The Old Salt Press also publishes Rick Spilman’s novel [*Hell Around the Horn*](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0091614MM/) and short story, [*Bloody Rain – Murder, Madness and the Monsoon*](http://www.amazon.com/Bloody-Rain-Madness-Monsoon-ebook/dp/B00BDAIDQ4/ref=la_B0091EL9SS_1_2?ie=UTF8&qid=1368617237&sr=1-2).

[Old Salt Press](http://oldsaltpress.com/), LLC is an independent press catering to those who love books about ships and the sea. We are an association of writers working together to produce the very best of nautical and maritime fiction and non-fiction. We invite you to join us as we go down to the sea in books.
