Here is a time-lapse video of the assembly of an ice sculpture of a tall-ship being at Pickering Wharf in Salem, MA last Saturday as part of the 11th annual [Salem So Sweet Chocolate and Ice Sculpture Festival](http://salem.patch.com/articles/salem-so-sweet-ice-sculptures-in-the-snow-video#video-13393577). Sadly, the ice ship was partially dis-masted, which is to say, [smashed by vandals](https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=618308418185408&set=a.159513787398209.35322.146960561986865&type=1&theater), on Saturday night. Fortunately, the video remains.
