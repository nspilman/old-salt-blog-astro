The British [Maritime And Coastguard Agency](http://www.mcga.gov.uk/c4mca/mcga07-home) has reported that the sail-training vessel, *[Fryderyk Chopin](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fryderyk_Chopin_\(ship\))*, has been secured to a buoy in Falmouth harbour, Cornwall, and the crew of 47, including 36 cadets, aged between 14 and 16, has been taken ashore.
