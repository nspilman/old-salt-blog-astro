![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/tallshipsamericabanner.jpg)Tomorrow, September 4th, a memorial service honoring the life of [Bert Rogers](http://www.tallshipsamerica.org/about/board/Rogers.php) will be held at 2PM on the North lawn at Fort Adams in Newport, RI.  Bert Rogers was the long-time Executive Director of [Tall Ships America](http://www.tallshipsamerica.org/) and an icon in the sail training community.
