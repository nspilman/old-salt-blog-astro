![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/wavertreesunset.jpg)If you are around New York harbor on Sunday, there is a wonderful opportunity to hear and join in singing sea chanteys as they were meant to be sung, onboard a windjammer. The chantey sing will aboard the restored 1885 built flagship of the seaport, [*Wavertree*,](https://southstreetseaportmuseum.org/visit/street-of-ships/wavertree/) at Pier 16 at the [South Street Seaport Museum](https://southstreetseaportmuseum.org/) in lower Manhattan at 2PM on Sunday, July 7th. Specially discounted tickets are available. [Click here to learn more.](https://www.facebook.com/events/2024259400988215/)

If you can’t make this Sunday’s event, there will also be chantey sings on the first Sunday in August and September on the Wavertree as well.
