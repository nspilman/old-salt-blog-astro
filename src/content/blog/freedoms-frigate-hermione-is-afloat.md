![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Hermione.jpg)We have [posted](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2012/03/22/the-two-frigates-hermoine-part-2-l-hermoine-1780-freedoms-frigate/)  last March about the replica of the [French light frigate *l’ Hermione*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_frigate_Hermione_\(1779\)), which has been under construction since 1997 at the historic dockyard in Rochefort, France.  The original *l’ Hermione* carried the 23 year old the Marquis de Lafayette back to America in 1780 carrying news of France support for the revolution.

This weekend, the replica *l’ Hermione* reached a major milestone. The *Hermione* is now afloat for the first time. The celebration of the ship’s launching is scheduled for July 6 – 8th.
