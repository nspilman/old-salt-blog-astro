The [USS *Kittiwake*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Kittiwake_\(ASR-13\)), a 1945-vintage submarine rescue ship, was scuttled this week off Grand Cayman’s Seven Mile Beach  in the Caribbean to become a destination for reef fish and sport divers.   The ship settled onto the bottom on an even keel in about 60 feet of water.
