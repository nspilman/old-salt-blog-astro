![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/12/51izzzstHzL._SL500_AA300_.jpg)Last June, we [posted about Jessica Watson’s book and album release](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/07/15/the-merchandising-begins-teen-sailor-jessica-watson-to-release-an-album/).   [Jessica Watson](http://www.jessicawatson.com.au/) is the now 17 year old Australian sailor who can rightly claim the title of the “youngest to sail solo and unassisted around the world.”    Now [Abby Sunderland](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/index.php?s=Abby+Sunderland),  who attempted to claim that title as well, but whose boat was dismasted in the Indian Ocean, has teamed up with Lynn Vincent to write a book of her own.   Vincent, was the ghost writer for Sarah Palin’s book, “[Going Rogue](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0061939897?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0061939897).”    Sunderland’s book will be titled, *[Unsinkable: A Young Woman’s Courageous Battle on the High Seas](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1400203082?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=1400203082)*.   It will be available this spring, but can be pre-ordered from Amazon.
