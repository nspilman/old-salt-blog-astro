Here is some glorious drone footage of the [Sailing School Vessel (SSV) *Oliver Hazard Perry*](https://www.ohpri.org/ship) transiting the Cape Cod Canal. At 207 feet long, the SSV *Oliver Hazard Perry* is the largest civilian Sailing School Vessel in the United States and is the first ocean-going full-rigged ship to built in the U.S. in over 100 years. Her accommodations hold 32 people overnight in addition to her 17 professional crew.
