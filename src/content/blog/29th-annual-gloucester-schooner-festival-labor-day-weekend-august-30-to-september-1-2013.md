The [29th Annual Gloucester Schooner Festival](http://gloucesterschoonerfestival.net/), in all its glory, is being held again over Labor Day weekend with waterfront tours, public lobster bakes, fireworks, and of course, many lovely schooners. Sunday begins with a parade of sail before the beginning of the start of Mayor’s Race for the Esperanto Cup, Ned Cameron Trophy and Betty Ramsey Trophy off Eastern Point.
