In 1606, Captain Willem Janszoon, in command of the Dutch ship *[Duyfken](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Duyfken),* became the first European to discover Australia. Now a replica of the [*Duyfken* has completed a voyage to commemorate the 400th anniversary](http://www.duyfken.com/voyages/2016-Hartog-400th-Anniversary-Voyage/2016_Hartog_400th_Anniversary_Voyage) of [Dutchman Dirk Hartog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dirk_Hartog) and his crew, the first Europeans to land on Western Australian. Hartog came ashore on an island just north of Shark Bay on October 25, 1616.  Here is a short video about the [*Duyfken*](http://www.duyfken.com/building-the-replica/replica)‘s commemorative voyage.
