Her French competitors refer to her as *La Petite Anglaise.*  Tomorrow, when the twenty boats in [Vendée Globe](http://www.vendeeglobe.org/en/) non-stop round-the-world single-handed yacht race set off from Les Sables-d’Olonne, France, [Samantha Davies](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samantha_Davies) will be the only woman in the race.  When she sails tomorrow, she will be waving goodbye to her husband and her Romain, and 14-month-old son, Ruben.   Davies is an experienced single handed sailor, placing fourth on corrected time in the [2008-2009 Vendée Globe](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vend%C3%A9e_Globe#2008-2009) as well as sailing in numerous other major races.
