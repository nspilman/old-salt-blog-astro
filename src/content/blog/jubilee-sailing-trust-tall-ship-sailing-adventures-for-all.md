A very nicely done video showing the inspiring work being done the [Jubilee Sailing Trust](http://www.jst.org.uk ).  The Jubilee Sailing Trust  is a registered charity that owns and operates the tallships *Lord Nelson* and *Tenacious,* the only two tall ships in the world designed and built to enable people of all physical abilities to sail side-by-side as equals.
