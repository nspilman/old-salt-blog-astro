![elfaro2](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2015/10/elfaro2.jpg)In a statement this morning, the US Coast Guard says that they believe that [*El Faro*,](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2015/10/roro-cargo-ship-el-faro-still-missing-with-33-crew-in-hurricane-joaquin/)  which disappeared last Thursday while blacked out in the path of Category 4 Hurricane Joaquin, has sunk. Coast Guard Capt. Mark Fedor said that they are now focusing on any signs of survivors. [Searchers have found life rafts and survival suits, including one survival suit with human remains.](http://www.cnn.com/2015/10/05/us/el-faro-missing-ship-hurricane-joaquin/)
