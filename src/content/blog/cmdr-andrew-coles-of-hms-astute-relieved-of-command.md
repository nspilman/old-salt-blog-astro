Not a great shock, but Cmdr. Andrew Coles has been relived of his command of  *[HMS Astute](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Astute_\(S119\))*, the Royal Navy’s newest and “stealthiest” submarine which very publicly  [ran aground last month near the Skye bridge off the Isle of Skye](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/10/23/hms-astute-update/).

[Officer relieved of command over grounded sub](http://edition.cnn.com/2010/WORLD/europe/11/27/uk.nuclear.submarine/?hpt=T2)

*“Commander Coles will continue to serve in the Royal Navy and he will be reappointed to a post where his talents and experience can be used to best effect,” a ministry statement said. “A new commanding officer will be appointed as soon as possible.”*
