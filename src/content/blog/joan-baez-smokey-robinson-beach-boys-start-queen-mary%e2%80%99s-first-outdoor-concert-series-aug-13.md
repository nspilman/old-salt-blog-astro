![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/08/dnc-queenmary.jpg)Folk music legend Joan Baez and special guest Roger McGuinn will kick off the first of the “[Summer Concert Series at the *Queen Mary*](http://www.queenmary.com/index.php?page=concert)” this Friday, August 13th,  as part of the summer-long [2010 Long Beach Sea Festival](http://www.longbeachseafestival.com/).  This will be the first a series concerts planned for August and September at the Harry Bridges Memorial Park at the *[Queen Mary](http://www.queenmary.com/index.php)* including performances by Motown giant Smokey Robinson on Aug. 18, legendary Southern California rock band the Beach Boys on Aug. 19 and three-time Grammy®-Award winning folk-rock artist Lucinda Williams and special guest JP, Chrissie & The Fairground Boys on Aug. 26.

Thanks to [Alaric Bond](http://www.alaricbond.com/index.html) for passing on news of the event.
