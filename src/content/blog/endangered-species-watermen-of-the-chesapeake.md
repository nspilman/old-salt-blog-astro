The [Mariners Museum](http://www.marinersmuseum.org/) in Newport News, VA has a new exhibition: [Endangered Species – Watermen of the Chesapeake](http://www.marinersmuseum.org/exhibitions/waterman-exhibition),  featuring extraordinary B & W portraits of watermen who work the waters of the Chesapeake Bay.   For those of us not in the area, the website includes photography and video of the waterman, including clips from “[The Last Boat Out](http://seltzerfilmvideo.com/lbo/last_boat_out.html),” a documentary about waterman on the Chesapeake which aired on PBS last spring.
