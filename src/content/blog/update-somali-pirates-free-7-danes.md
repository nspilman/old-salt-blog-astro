![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/51335424_008182434-1.jpg)Last February [we posted that Somali pirates had hijacked a Danish sailboat with four adults and three children aboard](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/02/28/somali-pirates-seize-danish-family-including-three-children-in-indian-ocean/).   Yesterday, the Danish Foreign Ministry announced seven Danish citizens, including a family with three children had been released. It has been reported that the pirates were paid a $3 million ransom on Tuesday.
