[Forty six crew members are reported missing](http://www.allheadlinenews.com/articles/7018236399?46%20Still%20Missing%20As%20Cause%20Eyed%20In%20South%20Korean%20Navy%20Ship%20Explosion) from the South Korean Cruiser *Cheonan* which sank late Friday.    South Korean officials are all but [ruling out direct North Korean involvement](http://news.asiaone.com/News/Latest%2BNews/Asia/Story/A1Story20100328-207187.html) in the sinking.   Fifty eight of the crew were rescued.
