On June 8th, the restored 1883 built [pilot schooner *No.5 Elbe* sank](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2019/06/pilot-schooner-no-5-elbe-ex-wander-bird-sinks-after-collision-on-river-elbe/) following a collision with the container ship *Astrosprinter.*  Salvors have now refloated the historic schooner using airbags and pumps and will now assess the extent of the damage. Images from the [Pressure Drop forum](http://www.pressure-drop.us/forums/content.php?9299-Wanderbird-Floats-Again!).
