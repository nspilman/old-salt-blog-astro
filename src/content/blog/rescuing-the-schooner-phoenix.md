![phoenix](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/phoenix.jpg)On October 9th, the [schooner *Phoenix*](https://www.facebook.com/SchoonerPhoenix?fref=ts) dragged her anchor in a storm in Port Townsend harbor, Washington. She ended up sinking, holed on the rocks of the breakwater. The good news is that, after a week underwater, the schooner was been refloated and has been hauled out in Port Townsend.  The *Phoenix’s* owner John Kruse is now figuring out how best to repair the schooner, including how he will finance it.  He is using [GiveForward.com](https://www.giveforward.com/fundraiser/mxcb/the-schooner-phoenix-rising-fund-james-kruse-s-boat-sunk-?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=fb_sharer&utm_campaign=php_fundraiser_main-muppets&og_action=hug&fb_ref=sharer-muppets&t=3) to attempt to raise the funds to put the schooner back in service.
