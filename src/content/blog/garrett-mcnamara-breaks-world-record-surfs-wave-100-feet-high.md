The Hawaiian surfer Garrett McNamara is said to have broken his own world record for the largest wave surfed when he caught [a wave reported to be around 100ft](http://www.surfertoday.com/surfing/8441-has-garrett-mcnamara-ridden-the-100-foot-wave-in-nazare) off the coast of Nazaré, Portugal.  McNamara set the previous record in 2011 also off Nazaré.
