Belfast hosted the [Tall Ships Belfast 2015](http://tallshipsbelfast.com/) from July 2-5. It sounds like a great success. As reported by [the Belfast Telegraph](http://www.belfasttelegraph.co.uk/video-news/video-tall-ships-highlights-from-four-fabulous-days-docked-in-belfast-31357482.html#.VZ4dfgORIgY.email): *The fleet of 46 sailing vessels drew record crowds to Belfast, making it one of the biggest events to be staged in the province. During four fabulous days docked in Belfast one ship recorded its highest ever number of visitors. Brazilian vessel, [Cisne Branco](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cisne_Branco), had 32,000 visitors on board during its stay in Belfast, the highest number it has recorded anywhere in the world.* Thanks to Irwin Bryan for passing along the news.
