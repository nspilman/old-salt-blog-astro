A 25 foot long baby humpback whale washed ashore on Jones Beach, on Long Island, New York yesterday morning.  There were no immediate signs of injury.  A necropsy will be performed today to determine what killed the whale.  In April, another [baby humpback died when it was stranded on a beach](http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/baby_whale_stranded_on_east_hampton_RLLsOG53i9ywxLPuMglALJ) in East Hampton,  Long Island.
