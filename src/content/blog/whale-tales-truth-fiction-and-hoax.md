[![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/07/ht_whale_onto_boat_100721_ssh-150x116.jpg)](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/07/ht_whale_onto_boat_100721_ssh.jpg)There has been considerable discussion whether the photo of a Southern Right whale breaching and landing on a sailboat off South Africa is real or Photoshopped.  (See the comments on our [previous post](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/07/21/breaching-southern-right-whale-crushes-sailboat/).)    A video has now emerged which shows the whale approaching breaching and landing on the sailboat.    We are going to cast our vote that the event was not Photoshopped.

In a related vein, the folks at [Yachtpals blog](http://yachtpals.com/) have a wonderful roundup of a variety of “whale tales” documented by photographic or video evidence.   Remarkably of the seven tales they highlight, five are true.

Check it out on the Yachtpals blog:  [Whale Tales – Truth, Fiction, and Hoax](http://yachtpals.com/boat-whale-9117)
