Last August, [we posted about the collision](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/08/12/the-sinking-of-the-msc-chitra-port-re-opens-while-the-finger-pointing-intensifies/) of the [containership *MSC Chitra*](http://www.shipspotting.com/modules/myalbum/photo-48394-MSC+CHITRA) and the [bulk carrier *Khalijia 3*](http://www.lloydslist.com/ll/sid/vessel/article174015.ece) which resulting in the sinking of the *Chitra* with a significant oil spill, a loss of cargo containers and the blockage of the port of Mumbai for five days.   [Maasmond Maritime’](http://www.maasmondmaritime.com/Default.aspx)s Clipping Service recently publish dramatic photos of the scuttling of the MSC *Chitra*, after a six day tow to deep water by Titan Maritime approximately 385 miles off of the coast of Mumbai, India.
