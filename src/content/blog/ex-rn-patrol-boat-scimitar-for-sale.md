![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/scimitar.jpg)Was there something missing from your stocking this holiday season? Perhaps the used, but still in good shape, Royal Navy patrol boat of your dreams? Then you could be in luck. [Alaric Bond](https://www.alaricbond.com/) was kind enough to pass along an [E-Bay classified ad for *Scimitar*](https://www.ebay.co.uk/itm/295426662780?mkcid=16&mkevt=1&mkrid=711-127632-2357-0&ssspo=7Bkot4zdRV6&sssrc=2349624&ssuid=ZLzVUa2JTS-&var=&widget_ver=artemis&media=EMAIL) – a former Royal Navy patrol boat on offer for £134,995. The 16 m (52 ft 6 in) [*Scimitar* is one of two Scimitar-class](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Scimitar_\(P284\)) fast patrol boats that served in Northern Ireland in their early careers and as guard ships with the Gibraltar Squadron from 2003 to late 2020. [*Scimitar* and her sister vessel *Sabre* were decommissioned](https://www.royalnavy.mod.uk/news-and-latest-activity/news/2022/march/30/220330-hms-sabre-and-hms-scimitar-decommissioned-in-portsmouth-dockyard-ceremony) in Portsmouth on March 30, 2022.
