On Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, [a quote](https://www.brainyquote.com/quotes/quotes/m/martinluth132359.html):

*We may have all come on different ships, but we’re in the same boat now.*  
*Martin Luther King, Jr.*

Also [Commemorating Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr., Day: A Sailor’s Perspective](http://navymedicine.navylive.dodlive.mil/archives/4056)  
By Command Master Chief (SW/FMF) Rusty Perry, originally posted in [U.S. Navy Live](http://navymedicine.navylive.dodlive.mil/archives/4056), the official blog of the U.S. Navy Bureau of Medicine and Supply.

*“Human progress never rolls in on the wheels of inevitability. It comes through the tireless efforts and the persistent work of dedicated individuals…” -Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr., 1964*

Shipmates,

One of the pioneering minds of this past century penned those words nearly five decades ago, and while appropriate for that time in United States history – a time replete with internal strife and the onset of an overseas conflict which claimed the lives of nearly 60,000 U.S. service members, the Vietnam War – still ring true today.  [Read more here.](http://navymedicine.navylive.dodlive.mil/archives/4056)
