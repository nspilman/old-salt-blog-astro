[![phillycamdents](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/phillycamdents.jpg)Tall Ships Philadelphia – Camden](http://tallshipsphiladelphia.com/) is being held on June 25-28, 2015 on the Delaware River.  Normally I wouldn’t post about an event so far in the future, but the [tickets for the event have now gone on sale.](https://www.ticketfly.com/venue/8343-penns-landing/)  So far the tall ships lined up for the event include the French frigate [*L’Hermione*](http://www.hermione2015.com/), the USCGC barque [*Eagle*](http://www.uscg.mil/datasheet/wixtrain.asp), the Brazilian Navy [*Cisne Branco*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cisne_Branco), Philadelphia’s Official Tall Ship [*Gazela*](http://gazela.org/ships/gazela/), the globe wandering barque [*Picton Castle*](http://www.picton-castle.com/) and the schooner [*Serenity*.](http://www.sailyorktown.com/schooner-serenity.html) Quite an impressive line up for being six months in the future.
