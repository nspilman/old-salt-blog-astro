We are two years late in posting this. Nevertheless, it seems like a worthwhile bit of silliness for a Friday afternoon.  Back in 2020, a deadly virus was running rampant across the globe. Performance venues and even rehearsals were shut down. But – the members of the shanty and sea song-singing group [Bounding Main](https://www.youtube.com/@BoundingMain/about) knew that “Life … finds a way.”  Here is their pandemic T-Rex quintet singing the shanty “Strike the Bell.”
