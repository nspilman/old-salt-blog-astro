A quirky video for a Saturday. The [mantis shrimp](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mantis_shrimp) is an intriguing, colorful and frankly, rather frightening creature. Fortunately, most types grow no more than about 4″ long, although the [zebra mantis shrimp](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lysiosquillina_maculata) found from East Africa to the Galápagos and the Hawaiian Islands can often grow to 15″ and an [18″ monster](http://www.iflscience.com/plants-and-animals/oversized-alien-shrimp-caught-florida-ided-currently-being-written-ls/) was caught off Ft. Peirce, FL in 2014.
