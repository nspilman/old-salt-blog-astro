A wonderful video promoting the [Mystic Seaport Museum](https://www.mysticseaport.org)‘s Annual [WoodenBoat Show](https://www.mysticseaport.org/event/the-woodenboat-show/), which began yesterday and runs through Sunday, July 2nd. There are more than 100 traditional and classic wooden boats of every type on display on the historic museum waterfront. The festival, which is hosted in partnership with WoodenBoat Publications, celebrates the design and craftsmanship of wooden craft.
