The freighter [*Amurskaya*](http://www.shipspotting.com/gallery/photo.php?lid=1616635), with a crew of of between 8 and 11, and carrying 700 tons of gold ore has been reported missing in stormy seas near the Shantar Islands, in the Sea of Okhotsk, on Russia’s Pacific Coast.   The [gold ore is estimated to be worth roughly $800,000.](http://www.telegraph.co.uk/finance/personalfinance/investing/gold/9641119/Polymetal-loses-700-tonnes-of-gold-ore-after-ship-goes-missing.html)   The general cargo vessel *Amurskaya* IMO 7313614, was built in 1973.  Thanks to Phil Leon of contributing to the post.
