[Fleet Week 2015](http://www.fleetweeknewyork.com/fleetweeknewyork/) kicked off with with a parade of three U.S. Navy ships, four U.S. Naval Academy yard patrol boats, and two U.S. Coast Guard cutters. More than 1,800 servicemen and women are participating, and [activities and events](http://www.fleetweeknewyork.com/fleetweeknewyork/events.html) will last through next Monday.
