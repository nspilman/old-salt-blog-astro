The pinky [schooner Ardelle](http://schoonerardelle.com/) was launched recently from the historic [Burnham Boatbuilding yard](http://schoonerardelle.com/images-of-the-burnham-boatyard) in Essex, MA.  They used the traditional “[Essex side-launch](http://schoonerardelle.com/the-launching-of-a-new-schooner)” technique, which, to my uninitiated eye, looks very odd.  Nevertheless, it obviously is effective.
