A deficiency of vitamin C from green leafy vegetables caused the dreaded sailor’s sickness scurvy.   A vertical garden installed on the *[Plastiki,](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/index.php?s=Plastiki)* which sailed this week on its 3-month voyage across the Pacific, may provide fresh greens for the six person crew while at sea.
