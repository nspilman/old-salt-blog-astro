Here is a cute trailer announcing the reopening of the composite clipper ship *[Cutty Sark](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cutty_Sark)* in her drydock in Greenwich, UK.  The ship built in 1869, caught fire on May 21, 2007  and has undergone a full restoration. The ship is due to reopen on April 26th.
