Last night, James Cameron became the first person ever to dive in a one man submarine to the very bottom of the ocean, the [Challenger Deep](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Challenger_Deep) in the Mariana Trench, 35,755 feet (5.8 nautical miles) below the surface.  He is only the third person to  ever dive the Challenger Deep and is the first person in over fifty years to do so.  A short video of his return to the surface following his epic voyage to the absolute bottom:
