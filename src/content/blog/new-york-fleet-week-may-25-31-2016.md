![fleetweekbanner](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2016/05/fleetweekbanner.jpg)Starting Wednesday this week, the fleet will be in town, well part of it anyway, to celebrate the 28th observance of [Fleet Week New York](http://www.fleetweeknewyork.com/fleetweeknewyork/index.html).  Eight Navy warships, two Coast Guard cutters, and a Marine expeditionary unit will converge on the harbor. An estimated 4,500 sailors, Marines, and Coast Guardsmen, as well as Royal Canadian sailors and Naval Academy midshipmen, will be participating. The festivities kick off with the [Parade of Ships](http://www.fleetweeknewyork.com/fleetweeknewyork/events.html) on Wednesday, followed by free ship tours at docks around the harbor. Click here to learn more about the [ships](http://www.fleetweeknewyork.com/fleetweeknewyork/ships.html) and [locations](http://www.fleetweeknewyork.com/fleetweeknewyork/events.html).
