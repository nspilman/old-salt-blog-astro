The crews sailing in the [Clipper Round the World Race](http://www.clipperroundtheworld.com/) represent over 40 nationalities. You can get a sense of the international makeup of the fleet by the banners and flags that they fly now that they have arrived in port.  Here is a short video I shot yesterday of the ten boat fleet in Liberty Landing marina in Jersey City, immediately across the Hudson River from lower Manhattan.

[Banners in the Breeze – Clipper Round the World Fleet at Liberty Landing, New York Harbor](http://youtu.be/u6NwACRMGqk)
