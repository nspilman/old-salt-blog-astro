![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2017/09/talesbanner1aweb.jpg)I will be participating in Jersey City’s [9th Annual Book Festival — Tales of Our Cities](https://www.facebook.com/events/113015326039754/permalink/117349658939654/?notif_t=like&notif_id=1505832497313554), in Van Vorst Park this Sunday. I will have a table with my books and books by other [Old Salt Press](http://oldsaltpress.com/) authors starting at 11AM. I will be reading from my new novel, [*Evening Gray Morning Red*](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B075NY1SRL/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1505822984&sr=1-1&keywords=spilman+evening+gray) at 3:30PM. I will also be giving away five Advance Reading Copies (ARCs) of the novel. Be sure to stop by and say hello if you are in the area.
