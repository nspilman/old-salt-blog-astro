After a stressful week or two, here is something that is just fun. A documentary crew on a [Pacific Offshore Expeditions](https://www.pacificoffshoreexpeditions.com/) outing last week, caught some [amazing footage of sea lions surfing](https://ftw.usatoday.com/2021/01/sea-lions-surf-giant-waves-in-awesome-display-caught-on-video) huge waves off Santa Barbara Island, California. The [Facebook version](https://www.facebook.com/pacificoffshore/videos/2617994618492068/), immediately below, is accompanied by the Surfaris’ hit, “Wipeout.” The [Instagram video](https://www.instagram.com/p/CJ-CssnpkCE/) (after the page break) has no sound.
