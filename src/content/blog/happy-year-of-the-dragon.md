Today begins the new lunar year – the [Year of the Black Water Dragon](The Year of the Black Water Dragon).  The lunar new year is celebrated for 15 days, from the first day of the first new moon of the year to the first full moon.  We wish everyone a most happy and prosperous New Year.  Thanks to [Frogma](http://frogma.blogspot.com/) for posting the link to the video of the underwater dragon dance.
