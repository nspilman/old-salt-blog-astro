Alaric Bond & Seymour Hamilton

[Seymour Hamilton](http://www.amazon.com/Seymour-Hamilton/e/B006N8SHSK/ref=sr_tc_2_0?qid=1361912372&sr=1-2-ent) recently sat down for a trans-Atlantic interview with [Alaric Bond](http://www.alaricbond.com/). They discussed Bond’s [Fighting Sail series](http://www.amazon.com/Alaric-Bond/e/B003WU39GW/ref=sr_tc_2_0?qid=1361912332&sr=8-2-ent) of novels, in particular, and about writing nautical fiction, in general.  It was a fascinating conversation. Seymour Hamilton is the author of the nautical fantasy series, The [Astreya Trilogy](http://astreyatrilogy.com/).  Alaric Bond has written five excellent novels in his [Fighting Sail](http://www.amazon.com/Alaric-Bond/e/B003WU39GW/ref=sr_tc_2_0?qid=1361912332&sr=8-2-ent) series. See our review of his latest – [Patriot’s Fate](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2012/09/the-patriots-fate-by-alaric-bond-a-review/).
