As we descend into the depths of Autumn, even if has been an unusually warm Autumn, here are two YouTube videos reminiscent of summer, when even blue whales and giant octopus can fly in the clear blue sky. In the first video, Randy Lowe, aka [Randy The Kite Man](https://www.facebook.com/Randy-the-kite-man-973638169365326/), flew a giant 100-foot blue whale kite over Delray Beach, Florida.  The video of the giant octopus kite is from Singapore where it took six people to get the huge kite airborne. [Read more about it here.](http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/giant-octopus-kite-at-marina-barrage-makes-waves-online)
