Last weekend, *[Oliver Hazard Perry](http://www.ohpri.org/)*, America’s newest and largest civilian sailing school ship sailed into Portland harbor in Maine on her maiden voyage, to participate in the [Tall Ships Portland 2015](http://www.pressherald.com/2015/07/19/festival-crowds-arrive-early-to-board-tall-ships-on-portlands-waterfront/) festival, which wrapped up yesterday.  Based in Newport, RI, the ship is the first ocean-going full rigged ship to be built in the United States in 110 years.  The video below from [WGME Channel 13](http://www.wgme.com/news/features/parade-of-sail-2015/stories/oliver-hazard-perry-largest-civilian-sailing-school-united-states-14.shtml#.Va4-ZvlVhBc) provides some great background and a tour of the new ship.
