![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2020/09/sfchantysing.jpg)We have [posted previously about New York’s South Street Seaport Museum](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2020/07/south-street-seaport-hosts-an-afternoon-of-online-sea-chanteys-sunday-july-5-202/)’s monthly virtual Sea Chanteys and Maritime Music events that take place on the first Sunday of every month. The next [Virtual Chantey Sing](https://www.facebook.com/events/170273521066986/?acontext=%7B%22source%22%3A%2229%22%2C%22ref_notif_type%22%3A%22event_aggregate%22%2C%22action_history%22%3A%22null%22%7D&notif_id=1599129628739295&notif_t=event_aggregate&ref=notif) is this Sunday from 2-4. [Click here to learn more or to download a free background for Zoom.](https://southstreetseaportmuseum.org/chanteysing/?fbclid=IwAR06pUgmy9YLanNUOo0hQQ0H-66e53gBp_qGyrJz-TbqO6_Xj7gKlPe6TSg)

Now, the West Coast is joining in the singing, as the [San Francisco Maritime Association](https://maritime.org/) is partnering with the SF Maritime National Historical Park for a virtual [Hyde Street Pier Sea Chantey Sing](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/hyde-street-pier-sea-chantey-sing-goes-virtual-tickets-117816224499) series to begin in mid-September. From their [events page](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/hyde-street-pier-sea-chantey-sing-goes-virtual-tickets-117816224499):

*On September 19, 2020, a 39-year SF singing tradition goes virtual! This folk music “landmark” on the historic ships at Hyde Street Pier now returns online, hosting singers from across the Bay Area – and the country. Visit [https://maritime.org/events-home/chantey-sing/](https://maritime.org/events-home/chantey-sing/) for details, including links to a signup sheet, info for lead singers, and some suggested virtual backgrounds. You should have a good internet connection and be ready to have fun!*
