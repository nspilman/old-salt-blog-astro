![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2011/06/ecolution_groot.jpg)Last December, [we posted about the sinking of Dutch scientist, Dr. Wubbo Ockles’  high tech yacht](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/12/02/correction-ecolution-innovative-sailing-yacht-owned-by-dr-wubbo-ockels-sinks/) *[Ecolution](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/12/02/correction-ecolution-innovative-sailing-yacht-owned-by-dr-wubbo-ockels-sinks/)* in Groningen.  The yacht was believed to have been sunk by vandals.  No arrests have been.  This week, the repaired *Ecolution* successfully  completed sea trials.  The [](http://www.victronenergy.com/upload/documents/Ecolution%20EN.pdf)*[Ecolution](http://www.victronenergy.com/upload/documents/Ecolution%20EN.pdf)* is designed with advanced power power systems to  generate  energy from water, wind and the sun.
