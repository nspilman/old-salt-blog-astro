Zeb Tilton was a legendary schooner captain from Martha’s Vineyard.  “[Zeb-Schooner Life](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001EXMPWS?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B001EXMPWS),” a documentary of his life and times is being screened tonight at 6:30 by the [National Maritime Historical Society](http://www.seahistory.org/html/home.htm) at the Hendrick Hudson Free Library in Montrose, NY.  Commentary will be provided by Peter Stanford, President Emeritus of the NMHS.   The documentary was produced by [Detrick Lawrence Productions](http://www.zebtilton.com/) of Edgartown, MA, based on the book , [Zeb, Celebrated Schooner Captain of Martha’s Vineyard](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0762738421?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0762738421), by Polly Burroughs. The film includes historic footage and photographs of Tilton and of his iconic schooner the *[Alice S. Wentworth](http://www.zebtilton.com/wentworth.html)*.
