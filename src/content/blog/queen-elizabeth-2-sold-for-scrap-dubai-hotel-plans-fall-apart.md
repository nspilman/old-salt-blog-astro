![que2x](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2012/12/que2x.jpg)The [MS Queen Elizabeth 2](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Queen_Elizabeth_2), which was purchased back in 2008 for conversion to a luxury hotel in Dubai, has reportedly been sold for scrap to Chinese interests for £20 million.  The original conversion plans were scuttled by a credit crunch in Dubai shortly after the ship purchase. Last July [we posted about a more modest hotel conversion plan](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2012/07/dubai-unveils-more-modest-plans-for-qe2-hotel/) for the QE2, which has now also fallen through.
