[Barista Uno on the Marine Cafe blog](http://marine-cafe.com/the-dona-paz-tragedy-25-years-on/#.UNMhw2-_ErU), posted this morning about a sad anniversary. Twenty five years ago today, the passenger ferry [*Dona Paz*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MV_Do%C3%B1a_Paz) collided with the oil tanker MT *Vector* in the Philippines*.* With a likely death toll of over 4,000 people, the sinking of the *Dona Paz* was deadliest peacetime maritime disaster in history.
