![weymouthlev](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/weymouthlev.jpg)I am on my way to the [Weymouth Leviathan](http://weymouthleviathan.org.uk/), a maritime literary festival on the south-west coast of England. I am looking forward to seeing old friends, as well as meeting many fine writers who I know only through the magic of the internet. I also hope to swing by Bristol and Southhampton to visit several historic ships that I have admired from afar.
