![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/06/ssrobin.jpg)The [](http://www.ssrobin.com/index.php)*[SS Robin](http://www.ssrobin.com/index.php)*, built in 1890, is the last remaining steam coaster in the world. She will soon be moved to a custom built pontoon barge which will support the old ship and serves as space for a floating museum.  The pontoon recently arrived in Lowestoft in the UK.   The *SS Robin* is expected to be lifted onto the pontoon on June 28th.  Thanks to [Alaric Bond](http://www.alaricbond.com/index.html) for the heads up.
