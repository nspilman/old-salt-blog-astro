![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/spotz.jpg)Last December we [posted](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2009/12/20/katie-spotz-will-attempt-to-cross-an-ocean-in-a-rowboat/) about Katie Spotz’s attempt to row across the Atlantic alone.  Yesterday she arrived in Georgetown, Guyana, in South America, after 70 days 5 hours 22 minutes in the Atlantic. Spotz, 22, is now the youngest person to cross an ocean in a rowboat, and the first American to row solo from mainland to mainland.
