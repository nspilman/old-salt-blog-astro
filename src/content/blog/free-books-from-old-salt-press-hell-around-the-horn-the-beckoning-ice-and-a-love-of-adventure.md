[![osplogo2thumbbw copy](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2013/05/osplogo2thumbbw-copy.png)](http://oldsaltpress.com/)To celebrate the upcoming publication of Joan Druett’s *Promise of Gold* trilogy, [Old Salt Press](http://oldsaltpress.com/) will be running a free book promotion for Rick Spilman’s [*Hell Around the Horn*](http://www.amazon.com/Hell-Around-the-Horn-ebook/dp/B0091614MM/ref=pd_rhf_gw_p_t_2_1EE7), and Joan Druett’s [*The Beckoning Ice*](http://www.amazon.com/Beckoning-Wiki-Coffin-Mysteries-ebook/dp/B00AHJI8P8/ref=la_B000APKEAQ_1_21?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1368623261&sr=1-21) and [*A Love of Adventure*](http://www.amazon.com/A-Love-of-Adventure-ebook/dp/B008988BIS/ref=la_B000APKEAQ_1_20?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1368623261&sr=1-20) over the next two weeks. Starting today May 15th and running through Sunday, May 19th, [*Hell Around the Horn*](http://www.amazon.com/Hell-Around-the-Horn-ebook/dp/B0091614MM/ref=pd_rhf_gw_p_t_2_1EE7) will be free on Kindle. From May 18 – 20, [*The Beckoning Ice*](http://www.amazon.com/Beckoning-Wiki-Coffin-Mysteries-ebook/dp/B00AHJI8P8/ref=la_B000APKEAQ_1_21?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1368623261&sr=1-21) will be free and from May 21-23, *[A Love of Adventure](http://www.amazon.com/A-Love-of-Adventure-ebook/dp/B008988BIS/ref=la_B000APKEAQ_1_20?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1368623261&sr=1-20)* will be also be free.

To read the books, you do not need a Kindle e-reader. With the [free Kindle app](http://www.amazon.com/gp/feature.html?ie=UTF8&docId=1000493771), the books are readable on iPads, Blackberries, android tablets, smart phones and desktop and laptop computers. Help us celebrate by downloading free copies of these three wonderful books.
