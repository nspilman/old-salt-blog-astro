A wonderful video of the [Swedish ship *Götheborg*](http://www.soic.se/en/) sailing in the Roaring Forties. The ship is a replica of the [Swedish East Indiaman](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/G%C3%B6theborg_\(ship\)) of the same name which sank in 1747. The ship is described as the world’s largest operational wooden sailing vessel.
