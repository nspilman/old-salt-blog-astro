![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/USS-Monitor-clock-works.jpg)Thanks to [Alaric Bond](http://www.alaricbond.com/index.html) for passing along two articles about historic maritime clocks.   The conservators at the Mariner’s Museum have restored the [engine room clock from the *USS Monitor*](http://skipjacksnauticalliving.blogspot.com/2010/04/uss-monitor-engine-room-clock-conserved.html) which sank in 1862.   On the other side of the Atlantic, a [pocket watch belonging to the captain of the ill-fated Lusitania](http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/england/merseyside/8651321.stm) has been donated to Merseyside Maritime Museum.
