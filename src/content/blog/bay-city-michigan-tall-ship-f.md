![baycitytallships](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/baycitytallships.jpg)Bay City Michigan kicked off its sixth [Tall Ship Celebration](http://www.tallshipcelebration.com/#intro) yesterday, featuring a diverse mix of historic ships and replicas, including the [U.S. Brig *Niagara*](http://www.flagshipniagara.org/); the replica galleon *[El Galeon Andalucia](http://www.elgaleon.org/galleons.html);* the replica Viking longship *[Draken Harald Hårfagre](http://www.drakenexpeditionamerica.com/);*  the brigantines *[Pathfinder](http://www.torontobrigantine.org/)* and *[Playfair](http://www.torontobrigantine.org/the_ships);* and the schooners *[Pride of Baltimore II](http://www.pride2.org/), [When and If](http://sailwhenandif.com/), [Mist of Avalon](http://www.mistofavalon.org/), [Madeline](http://www.maritimeheritagealliance.org/madeline), [Denis Sullivan](http://www.discoveryworld.org/exhibits/sailing-vessel-denis-sullivan/), [Appledore IV](http://www.baysailbaycity.org/appledore/appledoreiv.htm)* and *[Appledore V](http://www.baysailbaycity.org/appledore/appledorev.htm).*  The Bay City Tall Ship Celebration is one stop on the [Tall Ships America](http://www.sailtraining.org/) [Tall Ship’s Challenge](http://www.tallshipcelebration.com/new-page-1) featuring port calls-in nine ports in the Great Lakes and St. Lawrence Seaway.

The Tall Ship Celebration also  features [arts, craft beer, food and activities for children](http://www.tallshipcelebration.com/activities/#activities-special-events) and coincides with the [International Maritime Music Festival](http://www.tallshipcelebration.com/international-maritime-music).  The Tall Ship Celebration continues through Sunday.
