![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/TallShips6_15.jpg)We had previously [posted about the fleet of  sailing ships visiting Toronto](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/07/01/toronto-hosts-tall-ships/) as part of the [Great Lakes United TALL SHIPS CHALLENGE](http://www.sailtraining.org/tallships/2010greatlakes/index.php),  coordinated by the [American Sail Training Association (ASTA)](http://www.sailtraining.org/) across all five Great Lakes.  The fleet has now moved on to Cleveland, Ohio.  More than 100,000 people are expected to attend the [Cleveland Tall Ships Festival](http://www.clevelandtallships.com/).  The festival starts today and will continue through Sunday.
