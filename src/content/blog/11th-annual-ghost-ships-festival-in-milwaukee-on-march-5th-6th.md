[11th Annual Ghost Ships Festival 2010](http://ghost-ships.org/)

The [Ghost Ships Festival](http://ghost-ships.org/) is Wisconsin’s largest trade show devoted to Scuba Diving and Great Lakes Maritime History. Exhibits, workshops, and presentations cover just about every aspect of Great Lakes maritime history and scuba diving.
