![quarterdeck-1](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/quarterdeck-1.jpg)There are two wonderful newsletters for keeping up with what is going on in the world of nautical fiction – *[Astrodene’s Nautical Fiction Log Book](http://www.historicnavalfiction.com/newsletter/editions/archive/view/listid-1-mailinglist/mailid-43-hnf-log-book-march-2013)*, sponsored by David Haye’s [Historic Naval Fiction](http://www.historicnavalfiction.com) website and *[Quarterdeck](http://www.mcbooks.com/pdf/QuarterdeckMarch2013.pdf)* sponsored by [McBooks Press](http://www.mcbooks.com/).   The March edition of *Quarterdeck* features George Jepson’s interview of author [Helen Hollick](http://www.helenhollick.net/) and [Julian Stockwin](http://www.julianstockwin.com/) writing about life aboard a man-of-war, while the March *Astrodene’s Nautical Fiction Log Book* features author and historian, [J. D. Davies](http://jddavies.com/), and provides a run down of the new releases in nautical fiction and non-fiction.  Both newsletters are free and well worth subscribing to.
