Last September, [PortSide New York](http://www.portsidenewyork.org/) mounted a [multi-media exhibit](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/08/24/portside-newyork-exhibit-the-maritime-role-in-new-york-on-911/) on the historic buoy tender, [Lilac](http://lilacpreservationproject.org/),  about the extraordinary and little-known maritime role played in the recovery from the attacks of 9/11, from the evacuation to refuse removal and rebuilding.  A video about the exhibit:

[Mariners Respond to 9/11- a PortSide NewYork Exhibit](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pjpWMOzwhkA&feature=player_embedded#!)
