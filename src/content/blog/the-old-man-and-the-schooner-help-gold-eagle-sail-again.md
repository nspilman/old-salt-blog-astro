[![goldeagel1jpg](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/goldeagel1jpg.jpg)Captain Ron Strathman](https://www.facebook.com/ron.strathman) has cruised the Sea of Cortez for the last 6 years in his 1977 wooden schooner *Gold Eagle*.  Last month, [Hurricane Odile](http://www.weather.com/news/weather-hurricanes/tropical-storm-hurricane-odile-pacific-mexico-20140910) slammed into Mexico’s Baja peninsula with reported winds reaching 100 knots.  At least five died in the storm and dozens of boats were sunk.  One of them was Strathman’s *Gold Eagle*.  He is now attempting to raise money to salvage and restore the schooner.   [Click here to learn more and to help a sailor who could use a hand.](https://fundrazr.com/campaigns/5ssRf)
