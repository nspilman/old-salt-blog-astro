![545725](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/545725.jpg)The Peruvian navy has laid the keel for its new sail training ship, [*La Union*](http://elcomercio.e3.pe/66/doc/0/0/5/4/5/545722.pdf), at the Marine Industrial Services (Sima) shipyard in Callao. When completed in 2015, the ship will be the largest sail training vessel in South America. The ship will be four masted, with a sparred length of 113.5 m, a sail area of 3,500 m2, and will displace 3,500 tons.
