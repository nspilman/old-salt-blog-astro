[Oliver Hazard Perry](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oliver_Hazard_Perry)‘s message to his superiors was brief: “We have met the enemy and they are ours; two ships, two brigs, one schooner and one sloop.”  Perry’s victory at the [Battle of Lake Erie](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_lake_erie) on September 10, 1813, was one of the most strategically important naval battles of the War of 1812.   Starting on Thursday, a [thirteen day commemoration](http://battleoflakeerie-bicentennial.com/) of his victory will be held at site of the battle at Put-In Bay, Ohio. [Click here to learn more](http://battleoflakeerie-bicentennial.com/).  A short video of featuring the [Brig Niagara](http://www.flagshipniagara.org/).
