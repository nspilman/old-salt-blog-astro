In 1780, the French frigate, *l’Hermione* carried the Marquis de Lafayette to America with the news of French support for the American revolution. Now a replica *l[’Hermione](http://www.hermione2015.com/index.html)* has arrived in Yorktown, VA, the first of twelve ports that the frigate will visit on her reeanctment of her namesake’s historic voyage.
