After almost 51 years in service, twice its expected lifespan, the nuclear powered aircraft carrier [USS *Enterprise*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Enterprise_\(CVN-65\)) is winding up its 25th deployment and will be heading back to Norfolk to be deactivated on December 1 and decommissioned once all reusable items are removed.  USS *Enterprise* was the world’s first nuclear-powered aircraft carrier and is the second oldest commissioned vessel in the United States Navy, after the wooden-hulled, three-masted frigate [USS](http://www.history.navy.mil/ussconstitution/) *[Constitution](http://www.history.navy.mil/ussconstitution/).*
