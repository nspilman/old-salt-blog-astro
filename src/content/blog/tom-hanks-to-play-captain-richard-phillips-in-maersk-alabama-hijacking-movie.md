![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2011/03/tomhanks-100x147.jpg)It is being reported that [Tom Hanks](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000158/) will play Captain Richard Phillips in a new movie about the [attempted hijacking of the *Maersk Alabama*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maersk_Alabama_hijacking) by Somali pirates in 2009.    The movie, based on Phillip’s book , [A Captain’s Duty: Somali Pirates, Navy SEALS, and Dangerous Days at Sea](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003WUYROU/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&tag=tholsabl-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B003WUYROU), is being produced by Kevin Spacey and the team from “The Social Network.”
