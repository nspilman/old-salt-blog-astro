![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2013/08/Final_4x6_TugRace_Pcard_Pag.jpg)Great activities on the water, on both coasts, this Labor Day weekend. In San Diego, the annual [Festival of Sail](http://www.sdmaritime.org/festival-of-sail/) began yesterday with a Parade of Sail and continues through Monday with lots of great activities. as described by [San Diego Magazine](http://www.sandiegomagazine.com/calendar/index.php/name/Festival-of-Sail-2013/event/22433/), “*Festival of Sail is the LARGEST Tall Ship festival on the west coast and is hosted at the Maritime Museum of San Diego, transforming the North Embarcadero into a nautical theme park for 4 adventure filled days.*” [Click here to learn more.](http://www.sdmaritime.org/festival-of-sail/)

On the banks of the Hudson River in New York City, the [Working Harbor Committee](http://workingharbor.com/) is sponsoring the [21st Annual Great North River Tug Boat Race and Competition](http://workingharbor.com/tugrace_home.html) on Labor Day, September 2.   A Parade of Tugs passes by Pier 84 at 10:00 AM with the race starting at 10:30.  [Click here to learn more](http://workingharbor.com/tugrace_home.html).
