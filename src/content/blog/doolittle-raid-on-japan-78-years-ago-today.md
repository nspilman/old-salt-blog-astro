On April 18, 1942, sixteen B-25B Mitchell medium bombers took off from the U.S. Navy’s aircraft carrier [USS *Hornet*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Hornet_\(CV-8\)) deep in the Western Pacific Ocean to attack Toyko and other targets, in what would become known as the [Doolittle Raid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doolittle_Raid).  Here is a short video about the first airstrike on Japan in World War II.
