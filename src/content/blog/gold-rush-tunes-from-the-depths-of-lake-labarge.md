We have posted previously about the discovery of the Yukon Gold Rush iron sternwheeler *[A.J. Goddard](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/index.php?s=A.J.+Goddard+)* which sank in a storm in Lake Labarge in October of 1901.  Now with the discovery of a gramophone and three recordings, the music of the Gold Rush has literally risen from the depths of the lake.  To listen to a recording of the [Rendez Vous Waltz, recovered from the *A.J. Goddard,* click here.](http://www.cbc.ca/video/player.html?clipid=1824737632&position=0.005&site=cbc.ent.ca&zone=shows)
