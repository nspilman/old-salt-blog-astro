Schooner Shearwater

Father’s Day is celebrated in the United States on Sunday, June 16th.  Here are a few selected Father’s Day sails which most salty dads are likely to enjoy:

New York Harbor – [Schooner *Shearwater*, Father’s Day weekend](http://www.shearwatersailing.com/fathers-day-sail.cfm) – “Fathers will be offered drinks unlimited beer & wine for the duration of the sail. – “Shearwater Loves You Dad!”

New York Harbor – [*Clipper City* Craft Beer Tasting Sails](http://www.manhattanbysail.com/Craft-Beer-Tasting-Sails/) – Set sail on New York’s largest sailing vessel with the craft beer experts from [Urban Oyster](http://www.urbanoyster.com/), and get introduced to the wonderful new world of Craft Beer.

Perth Amboy, Raritan Bay – [Schooner *AJ Meerwald*](http://www.ajmeerwald.org/) – Father’s Day Pancake Sail 10AM – “Instead of breakfast in bed, surprise Dad with ‘breakfast on board’ cooked by the Meerwald crew.”

San Diego – [*Californian* – the Maritime Museum of San Diego](http://www.sdmaritime.org/fathers-day-celebration-sail/) is hosting a Father’s Day celebration aboard the replica revenue cutter *Californian*

Sausalito, CA – [Schooner *Seaward*](http://events.sfgate.com/sausalito_ca/events/show/328966743-fathers-day-sail) – Father’s day harbor sails in San Francisco harbor

Gloucester, MA. – [Schooner](http://schoonerardelle.com/) *[Ardelle](http://schoonerardelle.com/) –* Father’s Day Special – reserve a spot for your father for free with at least one paying guest.

Camden, ME – [Schooner *Olad* and Sailing Vessel](http://www.maineschooners.com/blog/fathers-day-sails-aboard-schooner-olad-and-sailing-vessel-owl) *[Owl](http://www.maineschooners.com/blog/fathers-day-sails-aboard-schooner-olad-and-sailing-vessel-owl) –* Penobscot Bay Father’s Day sails

Newport, RI – [Schooner Adirondack II](http://www.zerve.com/SailNewport/Fathers) – Father’s Day Sails in Newport Harbor and Narragansett Bay
