Tug W.O. Decker

The [Waterford Tugboat Roundup 2010](http://www.tugboatroundup.com/) starts tomorrow with a Parade of  Tugs past the Albany, NY waterfront before all return to Waterford at the junction of the Hudson and Mohawk Rivers for a  full weekend of competitions and entertainment.   The [Roundup Favorite Tug of 2010 competition](http://www.tugboatroundup.com/vote-for-your-favorite-tug) is very tight with 2637 voted for the lovely [*W.O. Decker*](http://www.tug44.org/tugboats.trawlers/tug-w-o-decker/), 2343 for the Fireboat (and Honorary Tug) *[John J. Harvey](http://www.fireboat.org/),* with 2286 votes for the *Atlantic Hunter II*.
