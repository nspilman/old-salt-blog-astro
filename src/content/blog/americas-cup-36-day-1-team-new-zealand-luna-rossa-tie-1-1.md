![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2021/03/ac36day1.jpg)On the first day of the final races in Aukland, NZ, of the 36th sailing of the [America’s Cup](https://www.americascup.com/en/36-americascup), the defender [Emirates Team New Zealand](https://emirates-team-new-zealand.americascup.com/) and the challenger [Luna Rossa Prada Pirelli](https://www.lunarossachallenge.com/), each won one race, ending the day in a tie. Team New Zealand won the first race by a margin of 31 seconds, while Luna Rossa won the second narrowly with a lead of only seven seconds. The best of 13 race series starts again on Friday. Short video highlights of both races are beyond the page break.
