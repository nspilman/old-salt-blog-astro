HMS Astute

The [BBC is reporting](http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-hampshire-13014640) that one sailor has been killed and another seriously injured on the Royal Navy nuclear submarine *[HMS Astute](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Astute_\(S119\)).* A member of the crew is reported to have shot two of his fellow sailors before being overpowered.   HMS *Astute*, the Navy’s newest and most advanced submarine, is docked at Southampton’s Eastern Docks on a five-day official visit to the city.  The [UK Press  Association  is also  reporting](http://www.google.com/hostednews/ukpress/article/ALeqM5gPvdTCrAEnp3H9sS6lSXgF-3aMfw?docId=N0480441302266843313A) that Southampton City Council’s mayor, leader and chief executive were aboard the submarine at the time of the shooting but were uninjured.

[Last October we posted about HMS *Astute* running aground](HMS Astute) off Skye during a training mission.
