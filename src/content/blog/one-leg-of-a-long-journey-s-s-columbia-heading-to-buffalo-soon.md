![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/columbia.jpg)The 113 year old, [SS *Columbia*](https://sscolumbia.org/), the oldest remaining excursion steamship in the United States, is one leg closer in her long journey to restoration and operation. She has just completed a drydocking in Toledo, Ohio and is being prepared to move to to Buffalo, NY for further work. The plan is to restore the boat to her 1902 appearance with the goal of [operating the historic steamer on the Hudson River](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2014/09/historic-steamer-ss-columbia-coming-to-new-york/). The estimated $10-million restoration will take about five to 10 years to complete. [Click here to help bring this wonderful vessel back to its former glory.](https://sscolumbia.org/donate/)
