Since 1990, strange and wondrous new forms of life have been wandering the beaches of the Netherlands, walking on the wind. They are [Theo Jenson’s “strandbeests,](http://www.strandbeest.com/index.php)” self propelled kinetic skeletal sculptures of PVC and fabric. Now for the first time, the strandbeests have crossed the Atlantic and will be [appearing this afternoon](http://www.coolhunting.com/culture/miami-art-week-2014-theo-jansens-dream-machines) at [Art Basel — Miami Beach.](https://www.artbasel.com/miami-beach)
