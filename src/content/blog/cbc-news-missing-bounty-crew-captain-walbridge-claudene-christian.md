**UPDATE:** [Missing crew member, Claudene Christian, 42, was pulled from the water](http://thechronicleherald.ca/novascotia/156687-one-of-bounty-crew-confirmed-dead?utm_source=website&utm_medium=banner&utm_campaign=most_read) unconscious and later confirmed dead by the Coast Guard.  Captain Robin Walbridge is still missing.

[CBC News is reporting](http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/nova-scotia/story/2012/10/29/ns-hms-bounty-hurricane-sandy.html): *Claudia McCann, whose husband Robin Walbridge is the captain of the Bounty, told CBC News her husband is one of the two missing crew members. CBC News has learned the other missing crew member is Claudene Christian.*

[Claudene Christian is reported](http://southern-traveller.com/2012/05/19/the-tall-ship-bounty-visits-wilmington/) to be a distant relative of Fletcher Christian, the original Master’s Mate who seized command of the ship during the mutiny.
