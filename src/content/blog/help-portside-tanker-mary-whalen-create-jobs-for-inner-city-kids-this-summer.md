![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/080905_volies_paint_house_s.jpg)We recently [posted](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/05/25/congratulations-to-port-side-new-york-and-the-mary-whalen/) about the tanker *[Mary Whalen](http://www.portsidenewyork.org/mary_whalen.htm)* being deemed eligible to be listed on the National Register of Historic Places.   [PortSide New York](http://www.portsidenewyork.org/about.htm) and the *Mary Whalen* are also participating in New York City’s [Summer Youth Employment Program](http://www.nyc.gov/html/dycd/html/jobs/summer_youth_employment.shtml), which provides meaningful summer work for inner city youth between the ages of 14 and 24.  To support the program PortSide needs to raise $16,000 within the next month.  [](http://www.portsidenewyork.org/about.htm)[PortSide New York](http://www.portsidenewyork.org/about.htm) is a 501c3 non-profit organization.  All donations are tax deductible.
