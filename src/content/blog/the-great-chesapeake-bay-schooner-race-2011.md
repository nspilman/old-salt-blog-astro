Last Friday, 39 schooners set off from Baltimore, Maryland sailing 127 miles down the Chesapeake to Portsmouth, Virginia in the [22nd Annual Great Chesapeake Bay Schooner Race](http://www.schoonerrace.org/).  This year the first schooner to cross Thimble Shoal with an elapsed time of 23 hours and 36 minutes was the *[America 2.0](http://www.sail-nyc.com/schooner-america-2.0-sails)*, with Andrew Neuhauser at the helm.   The schooner *[Woodwind](http://www.schoonerwoodwind.com/)*, captained by Ken Kaye, won Class A honors, while [*Adventurer*, Mark Faulstick, captain](http://goodmorninggloucester.wordpress.com/2010/09/07/adventurer-capt-mark-faulstick-%E2%80%93-wins-small-schooners-race/), and the *Avelinda*, Diane Sternberg, captain, won Class B and C honors, respectively.
