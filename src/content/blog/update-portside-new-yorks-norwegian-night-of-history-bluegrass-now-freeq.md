![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/paradisemountainboys.jpg)We recently posted about [PortSide NewYork’s Norwegian Water Stories & a Night of Bluegrass](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2015/09/portside-new-yorks-norwegian-water-stories-a-night-of-bluegrass/), an event being held tomorrow, Thursday, September 24, 2015 from 7:00 PM to 11:00 PM at Atelier Roquette, 63 Commerce Street, Red Hook, Brooklyn, NY 11231.  The event is now **free**. (Those who have purchased tickets will be given refunds.) As we posted previously, [PortSide NewYork](http://portsidenewyork.org/) and the historic tanker [Mary A. Whalen](http://portsidenewyork.org/mary-a-whalen/) are hosting the NYC premier of the Norwegian bluegrass band, the [Paradise Mountain Boys](https://www.facebook.com/pages/Paradise-Mountain-Boys/156404511182422?sk=info&tab=page_info), as well as a multimedia presentation of Norwegian WaterStories. Sounds like a great. evening if you are in the area.  [Click here to learn more.](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2015/09/portside-new-yorks-norwegian-water-stories-a-night-of-bluegrass/)
