The [Australia Day Regatta](http://www.charterworld.com/news/175th-australia-day-regatta-huge-fleet-classic-yachts-grace-sydney-harbour) was held today in Sydney harbor and featured more than 50 classic wooden yachts, many gaff-rigged, and several built more than a century ago. The Australia Day Regatta is the oldest continuously-conducted annual sailing regatta in the world, having been held each year since 1837 to commemorate the anniversary of the first European settlement of Australia.
