Carter Fish Company by N. Rauam

A wonderful show by Naima Rauam.  It captures everything about the old market with the possible exception of the smell of fish.

[Fifth Annual Remembering Fulton Fish Market Art Exhibit 2010](http://artpm.com/shows.htm)

*Commemorating the fifth Anniversary of the Fish Market’s move from South Street to the Bronx. A fond look back to the time when the streets of the Seaport came alive every night with the lively commerce of buying and selling fish.*

*Watercolors and charcoals by Naima Rauam of old Fulton Fish Market on South Street*

*November 4 – 28, 2010 at 210 Front Street (corner of Beekman), South Street Seaport, NYC*

*Reception: Thursday, November 4, 5 – 8 pm*

*Exhibit open Tuesday–Sunday, noon to 7 pm (closed Thanksgiving Day) 212-964-8465*

*This exhibit is sponsored by Seaport Marketplace*
