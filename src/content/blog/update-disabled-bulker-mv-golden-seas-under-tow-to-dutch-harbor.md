Tor Viking

*[MV Golden Seas](http://www.allseas.gr/fleet_detail.cfm?ItemID=41),* a 74,475 DWT  bulk carrier, which suffered a turbocharger failure on its main engines and was in danger of drifting onto the Aleutian Islands, has been taken under tow by the tug/supply boat *[Tor Viking II](http://www.vikingsupply.com/fleet?page=show_vesselspec&vesselid=1)*.   The bulk carrier is being towed to Dutch Harbor, Alaska and is expected to arrive early Tuesday.  The  US Coast Guard Cutter *[Alex Haley](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USCGC_Alex_Haley_\(WMEC-39\))* is escorting the two vessels.
