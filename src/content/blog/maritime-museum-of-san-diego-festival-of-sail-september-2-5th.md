![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/resizedimage577242-fos-top-image3.jpg)This like a great weekend to be in San Diego.  OK, an especially great weekend as the Port of San Diego and the  [Maritime Museum of San Diego](http://www.sdmaritime.org/) are hosting a four day [Festival of Sail.](http://www.sdmaritime.org/festival-of-sail/)  Music food, sailing, mock gun battles, a parade of tall and historic ships.  Even the [Pacific Voyagers](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/08/03/polynesian-voyaging-canoes-arrive-in-san-francisco-through-the-golden-gate/) will be joining in.

[Festival of Sail 2011 will be Friday September 2nd thru Monday the 5th, 2011](http://www.sdmaritime.org/festival-of-sail/)
