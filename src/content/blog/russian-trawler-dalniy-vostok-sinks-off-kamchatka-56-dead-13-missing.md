[*![rtrawler](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rtrawler.jpg)Dalniy Vostok*](http://www.vesselfinder.com/vessels/BATM-DALNIY-VOSTOK-IMO-8730429-MMSI-273378520), a Russian freezer trawler, has sunk in the Sea of Okhotsk off the Kamchatka peninsula. Of the 132 people aboard, 56 are reported to have died, while 13 remain missing. Sixty-three people were rescued.  The trawler sank quickly at around 06:30 local time (20:30 GMT Wednesday) after flooding was reported in the engine room.  The ship sank without sending a distress call.  Seventy-eight of those on board were Russian and 42 were from Myanmar. The remainder were from Vanuatu, Latvia and Ukraine. Thanks to Phil Leon for contributing to this post.
