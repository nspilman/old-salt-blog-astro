The U.S. [Coast Guard](http://coastguard.dodlive.mil/index.php/2010/12/your-vote-counts-coast-guard-video-of-the-year/) is posting the top 11 rescue/mission videos of 2010.  Starting today they will be posting one video per day.  There are three ways to vote for your favorite video. Either “like” the video on the [Coast Guard Youtube channel](http://www.youtube.com/user/USCGImagery?feature=mhum#p/c/1127964652239861), “like” your favorite on the Coast Guard [Facebook fan page](http://www.facebook.com/uscoastguard) or leave a positive comment on the [Coast Guard Compass blog](http://coastguard.dodlive.mil/index.php/2010/12/your-vote-counts-coast-guard-video-of-the-year/) for that video.   In the mean time, here is a compilation of the eleven best videos.   [Click here to view the Day 1 Video – Coast Guard rescues father and son caught in surf.](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pwxV1B4oUlI) The professionalism, courage and skill of these Coast Guardsmen is absolutely breathtaking.
