![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/qyarterdeckwinter2020.jpg)The Winter 2020 edition of [Quarterdeck](https://globewebsites-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/QD%20WINTER%202020.pdf?fbclid=IwAR2wZ1L4JFMcqlH3-JSpghqxd-xeGg-aGnrmq8WC5tQ4thf_QPQo9sy5H04), hosted by [McBooks](http://mcbooks.com/), is out. If you like nautical fiction, the [quarterly e-journal edited by George Jepson](https://www.facebook.com/tallshipscommunications/?__tn__=%2Cd%2CP-R&eid=ARCFSNvKFby1hNaOBM1WVsA6ulMVOaBv06Z3OaNKz2KgUlM-n8KhWlxjfvEcSWyIXUpVqlwXpgFX6Lmy) is a treasure.

The Winter 2020 edition features an interview with [Alaric Bond](https://www.alaricbond.com/) and a review of the [*Hellfire Corner*](https://www.facebook.com/coastal.forces/?ref=search&__tn__=%2Cd%2CP-R&eid=ARCe4JqTiGiHXIZb4mAKN8nYGnX_XRe_bTKANaeJEXq4VYU8Fg6Mh1SDeJt_0WkakRLR4jYjQy7W8a13); the first book in the Coastal Forces series, due out in February. Additionally, [Chris Durbin](https://chris-durbin.com/) has an article, *The First Landing Craft, Purpose-built for British amphibious operations*, as well as a feature on his latest novel, *[Perilous Shore](https://www.amazon.com/Perilous-Shore-Holbrooke-Adventure-Adventures-ebook/dp/B08376WWM6/ref=sr_1_1?keywords=Perilous+Shore&qid=1578604335&s=digital-text&sr=1-1).* [Antoine Vanner](https://dawlishchronicles.com/)‘s new novel [*Britannia’s Innocent*](https://www.amazon.com/Britannias-Innocent-Dawlish-Chronicles-February-ebook/dp/B081S3N6CN/ref=sr_1_1?keywords=Britannia%E2%80%99s+Innocent&qid=1578604373&s=digital-text&sr=1-1) is also featured. Articles by Kathy Stockwin, *Kydd’s Portsmouth,* and Kim Reeman, *When Heroes Die,* are fascinating.

If you are not familiar with [Quarterdeck](https://globewebsites-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/QD%20WINTER%202020.pdf?fbclid=IwAR2wZ1L4JFMcqlH3-JSpghqxd-xeGg-aGnrmq8WC5tQ4thf_QPQo9sy5H04), it is definitely worth checking out.
