![rdpitts](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/rdpitts.jpg)Dutch artist Florentijn Hofman’s inflatable sculpture, [“Spreading Joy Around the World,” universally known simply as “Rubber Duck](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2013/06/spreading-joy-around-the-world-aka-rubber-duck-inflated-deflated-revived-censored/)” has arrived in Pittsburgh after spending the spring and summer on Hong Kong.  Pittsburgh will be the 14th city on the Rubber Duck’s world tour.  The [WTAE Channel 4 Action Cam caught the image](https://twitter.com/WTAE/status/383705548153028608/photo/1) of the 40-foot-tall, 30-foot wide Rubber Duck arriving under tow on the Allegheny River.
