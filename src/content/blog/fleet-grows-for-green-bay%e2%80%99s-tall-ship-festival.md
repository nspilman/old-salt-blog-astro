Pride of Baltimore II

There are twelve tall ships scheduled to participate in Green Bay, Wisconsin’s  Tall Ship festival this August, though at this point, only six names have been announced. These are the *[HMS Bounty](http://www.tallshipbounty.org/)*, the schooners *[Roseway](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roseway)*, *[Amistad](http://www.amistadamerica.org/)*, the *[Pride of Baltimore II](http://www.pride2.org/index.php)*, the *[Unicorn](http://www.tallshipunicorn.com/index.html)* and the *[S/V Denis Sullivan](http://open.salon.com/blog/rick_spilman/2010/02/22/S/V%20Denis%20Sullivan)*.
