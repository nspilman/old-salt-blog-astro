The [Space Shuttle Enterprise](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Enterprise) arrived in New York today on its way to the [Intrepid Sea, Air and Space Museum.](http://www.intrepidmuseum.org/)   The Shuttle Enterprise rode piggy-back on a NASA 747, flying a loop over the Hudson River before landing at Kennedy Airport.  The shuttle will then be moved onto a  barge for the last leg of the trip to the museum at the *[USS Intrepid](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Intrepid_\(CV-11\))* on the Hudson River in New York City.   A short video of the flyby:
