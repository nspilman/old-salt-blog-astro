A beautifully shot video of a beautiful ship, the three-masted top-sail schooner [*Oosterschelde*](http://www.oosterschelde.nl/en/). The schooner with a sparred length of 164′ is [described as](http://www.oosterschelde.nl/en/ship/) “the last remaining representative of the large fleet of schooners that sailed under the Dutch flag at the beginning of the 20th century. As the largest restored Dutch sailing ship the ‘Oosterschelde’ is a monument for Dutch shipbuilding and maritime navigation under sail.”
