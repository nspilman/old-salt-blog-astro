Around 1607, colonists in the Popham Colony built the [pinnace *Virginia*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virginia_\(pinnace\)), the first English-built ship in what is now Maine and possibly in all of the English-colonized areas of North America. Now over 400 years later, a [recreation of the *Virginia*](https://mfship.org/) is nearing completion in Bath, Maine.
