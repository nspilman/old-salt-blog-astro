A video for a cold winter day. We recently [posted about the exciting voyage plans](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2017/01/ssv-oliver-hazard-perry-bound-for-bermuda-florida-cuba-and-the-arctic/) for 2017 on the School Sailing Vessel (SSV) [*Oliver Hazard Perry*](http://www.ohpri.org/). This year, the ship will be sailing to Florida, Bermuda, Cuba and the Arctic. Here is a video of a teen voyage in August of 2016.

[A Week Aboard Oliver Hazard Perry](https://youtu.be/ithlwOSWxnQ)

*Oliver Hazard Perry* is a 207 feet long, steel hulled, three-masted square-rigged ship and is the largest privately owned tall ship, as well as the largest civilian sail training vessel in the United States. It is also the first ocean-going full-rigged ship to be built in the US in over 100 years. She is operated by the non-profit organization [Oliver Hazard Perry Rhode Island](http://www.ohpri.org/).
