Damage to Tanker Mindoro

This morning around 4:56 UTC the Cypriot container ship *[Jork Ranger](http://www.vesseltracker.com/en/Ships/Jork-Ranger-9322542.html)* collided with the [Greek tanker](http://www.marinetraffic.com/ais/default.aspx?zoom=10&mmsi=240924000&centerx=3.991427&centery=52.32441) *[Mindoro](http://www.marinetraffic.com/ais/default.aspx?zoom=10&mmsi=240924000&centerx=3.991427&centery=52.32441)*, carrying  jet fuel off the Scheveningen coast in the North Sea.  The collision caused a fuel spill from a five-meter wide puncture in the tanker.

[Oil Slick In North Sea After Tanker-container Ship Collision](http://www.rttnews.com/Content/MarketSensitiveNews.aspx?Id=1442446&SM=1)

*Reports quoting the Dutch Coast Guard said the spill did not cause any environmental damage, as a small trail of light paraffin was evaporating soon and drifting away from the Dutch shoreline.*

*The leak from a five-meter wide hole was plugged by rescue teams without much delay.*
