![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/niagara2.jpg)This is a bit last minute, but I see a [post on Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/flagshipniagara/posts/10157863201548639) about a free online sail training course presented by the [Flagship Niagara League](https://sailfnl.org/).

*Free Online Sail Training Course! Learn about how [Niagara](https://sailfnl.org/about-niagara/) is rigged and sailed!*

*Join Captain Cusson of the U.S. Brig Niagara for a six-week online sail training course. Starts Wednesday, February 3rd, at 6pm EST.*

*Visit [learnfnl.com](https://www.learnfnl.com/) to register!*

*The free online training course is designed to provide those with little or no experience in traditional sail an introduction to the terminology and concepts of a square-rigged sailing ship.*

*The U.S. Brig Niagara based in Erie Pa is the platform used to introduce concepts about how it is rigged and the procedures of sailing the vessel. Topics will include rigging, sails, sailing maneuvers, knots used on board, and more.*

*This course is free and open to anyone interested in participating. The course will be conducted online through Zoom, Wednesdays at 6pm EST with 6 sessions. [Registration is required](https://us02web.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_cpS71Ck7QLWDZTVwFwPtIg). February 3rd – March 10th, 2021.*
