A video shot and edited by Dave Elsmo about the *[Deuce](http://www.iceboat.org/wssa2/deuce/deuce.html)*, the world’s largest iceboat, on Lake Mendota, in Madison, WI during the [Wisconsin Stern Steerers Association](http://www.iceboat.org/wssa.html) Regatta. The 54′ stern-steerer speed demon built in the 1930s and rebuilt in 2006 is said to have a top recorded speed of 104 mph (90 knots.)
