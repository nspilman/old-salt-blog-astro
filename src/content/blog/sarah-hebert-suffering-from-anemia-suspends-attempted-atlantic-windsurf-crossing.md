On Monday [we posted about Sarah Hebert’s bold attempt to cross the Atlantic by windsurfer](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2012/03/05/sarah-hebert-sailing-across-the-atlantic-by-windsurfer/).  She has been battling 3-to-5 metre swells and constant 25-knot winds.  Now, suffering from extreme dizziness, diagnosed to be caused by anemia, she has suspended her attempt but vows to return to try again.
