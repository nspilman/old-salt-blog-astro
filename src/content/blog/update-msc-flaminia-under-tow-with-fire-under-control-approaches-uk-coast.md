![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Msc-Flaminia-693832.jpg)The fire on the containership *[MSC Flaminia](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2012/07/15/containership-msc-flamina-in-flames-in-the-north-atlantic/),* abandoned in the mid-Atlantic after a fire and explosion on July 15th, is reported now to be under control. The ship is under by the Smit salvage tug *Fairmount Expedition* at five knots and is roughly 320 NM off the coast of the the UK.  The tugs *Anglian Sovereign* and *Carlo Magno* remain on standby.  The *Anglian Sovereign* has been monitoring temperatures aboard the ship and will resume cooling as necessarily.  The ship and tugs are now in dense fog.  Once viability improves, a salvage team will go aboard the ship and inspect for burning containers and extinguish any fires found.
