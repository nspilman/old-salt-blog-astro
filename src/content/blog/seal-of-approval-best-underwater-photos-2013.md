Best Overall Photo 2013 University of Miami Underwater Photo Contest: Harbor seal in kelp forest photographed by Kyle McBurnie.

From more than 650 underwater images submitted, Kyle McBurnie’s photo of a harbor seal in a kelp forest at Cortes Bank was chosen as “Best Overall” photograph in the the [2013 Annual Underwater Photography Contest](http://www.rsmas.miami.edu/news-events/press-releases/2013/seal-of-approval/) hosted by the University of Miami (UM) Rosenstiel School of Marine & Atmospheric Science.  [Click here to view the winning photographs in all categories](http://www.rsmas.miami.edu/outreach/underwater-photography/2013-winners/).  Thanks to Irwin Bryan for passing along the news.
