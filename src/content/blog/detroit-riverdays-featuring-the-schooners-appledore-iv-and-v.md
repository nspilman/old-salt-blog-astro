The [Detroit Riversdays](http://www.riverdays.com/) Festival starts this evening and runs through Sunday. In addition to lots of music, food and riverside activities, the sister schooners, [85′ Appledore IV, and the 65′ Appledore V](http://www.baysailbaycity.org/appledore/appledore.htm) will be available for tours. Sounds like fun.
