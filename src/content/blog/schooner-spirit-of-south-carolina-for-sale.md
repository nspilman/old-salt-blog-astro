![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2011/12/157212153-O_001.jpg)Sad news from the Palmetto State. The [South Carolina Maritime Foundation](http://www.scmaritime.org/index.php) plans to try to sell the schooner *[Spirit of South Carolina](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/02/04/spirit-of-south-carolina-tall-ship-program/)* to pay off its mounting debts.  The foundation is reported to be being sued by a bank and a former landlord for more than $2 million in loans and thousands of dollars in back rent. Since 2007, over 9,500 students have participated in educational programs on the schooner.  See our previous post  – [Spirit of South Carolina Tall Ship program](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/02/04/spirit-of-south-carolina-tall-ship-program/).
