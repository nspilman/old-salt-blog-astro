[![duluth2016](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/duluth2016.jpg)](http://tallshipsduluth.com/)[Tall Ships Duluth 2016](http://tallshipsduluth.com/), billed as the “Greatest Spectacle on Lake Superior,” kicks off this morning at 11AM and runs through Sunday, August 21th. The festival features eight tall ships including [El Galeon *Andalucía*](http://www.elgaleon.org/galleons.html), the [US Brig *Niagara*](http://www.flagshipniagara.org/us-brig-niagara/flagship-niagara-about/), and the schooners [*Pride of Baltimore II*](http://www.pride2.org/), [*Mist of Avalon*](http://www.mistofavalon.org/), [*When and If*,](http://sailwhenandif.com/) [*Denis Sullivan*](http://www.discoveryworld.org/exhibits/sailing-vessel-denis-sullivan/), [*Appledore V*](http://www.baysailbaycity.org/appledore/appledorev.htm), and [*Zeeto*](http://www.schoonerman.com/zeeto.htm). The “[World’s Largest Rubber Duck](http://tallshipsduluth.com/attractions/worlds-largest-rubber-duck/)” will also make an appearance. The festival offers ship tours, day sails, educational programming, food and beverage, entertainment and fun for all ages.

This year, the festival has partnered with [Art in Bayfront Park](http://www.artinbayfrontpark.com/) to create an event within an event including 150+ high quality art vendors.
