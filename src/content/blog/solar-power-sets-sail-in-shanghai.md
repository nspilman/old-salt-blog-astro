![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/head-on-from-Luna-ParkII_LR.jpg)Last week at the [Shanghai World Expo](http://en.expo2010.cn/), the *SunTech Guosheng* solarsailor, an innovative 31.5 meter solar-powered passenger vessel sailed on its maiden voyage on the Huangpu River.  The vessels is owned by [Suntech Power Holdings](http://am.suntech-power.com/), the world’s largest producer of crystalline silicon solar panels, using designs and technology developed by Australia-based [Solar Sailor](http://www.solarsailor.com.au/media-100519-shanghai-media-release.htm).  The rigid solar panels also serve as sails.   Capable of carrying carry 180 passengers, the *Suntech Guosheng* solarsailor is the largest hybrid solar vessel built to date.
