For the second year running, in-person [Fleet Week in New York](https://www.timeout.com/newyork/fleet-week-nyc), traditionally beginning in the week before and running through the Memorial Day Weekend, was canceled due to the Covid-19 pandemic. A virtual Fleet Week celebration has been ongoing, however, on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/FleetWeekNewYork), [Twitter](https://twitter.com/FleetWeekNYC?ref_src=twsrc%5Egoogle%7Ctwcamp%5Eserp%7Ctwgr%5Eauthor), and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/fleetweeknyc/?hl=en).
