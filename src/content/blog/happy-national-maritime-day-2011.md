![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2011/05/National-Maritime-Day.jpg)Happy National Maritime Day!  The day has been celebrated in the US since 1933.  After World War II US flag shipping accounted for over 40% of the world’s total fleet.  Currently the US ranks around 10th in the world by number of ships, placing between Indonesia and Hong Kong.
