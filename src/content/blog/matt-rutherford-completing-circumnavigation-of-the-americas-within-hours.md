[![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2012/04/mattr2.jpg)Matt Rutherford](http://www.solotheamericas.org/), sailing *St. Brendan*, a 27′ Albin Vega, is within hours of sailing into the Chesapeake Bay, finishing his solo circumnavigation of the Americas!  Matt is planning to on setting foot on-shore for the first time in 25,000 miles-at-sea, at noon, Saturday, April 21 at Annapolis City Dock.  Our heartfelt congratulation on an amazing voyage. To learn more go to: [Solo Around the Americas](http://www.solotheamericas.org/)

Matt’s motivation for the trip was to show people, particularly those with disabilities, that there are no limits to what can be accomplished in life; and to raise money for [Chesapeake Region Accessible Boating (CRAB)](http://crabsailing.org/), a nonprofit sailing program for people with disabilities, based in Annapolis, Md.
