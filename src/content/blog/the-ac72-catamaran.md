The 72 foot long catamarans that will be competing for the [America’s Cup](http://www.americascup.com) in San Francisco in 2013 are both breathtakingly beautiful and slightly terrifying.  Powered by a towering wing sail and well capable of sailing at the twice the speed of the wind, these high-tech monsters suggest the still untapped potential of sail.  A 3d animated fly-around of the [AC72](http://www.americascup.com/en/Discover/Boats/The-AC72-catamaran/):
