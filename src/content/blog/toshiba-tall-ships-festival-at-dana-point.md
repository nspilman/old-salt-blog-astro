![danapointfestival](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/danapointfestival.jpg)The [Ocean Institute](http://www.ocean-institute.org/index2.html) is celebrating its 29th year hosting the [Toshiba Tall Ships Festival](http://www.tallshipsfestival.com/) at Dana Point, CA.  The festival kicks off with a tall ships parade at sunset tomorrow, September 6th with festivities continuing through the weekend.  Participating ships include the brig [*Pilgrim*](http://www.ocean-institute.org/programs/pilgrim.html), the topsail schooner [*Spirit of Dana Point*,](http://www.ocean-institute.org/programs/spirit.html) the twin brigantines *[Irving Johnson and Exy Johnson](http://www.brigantines.com/)*, the three masted schooner *[American Pride](http://www.americanpride.org/)*, the topsail schooner [*Californian*](http://www.sdmaritime.org/californian/), the schooner *Curlew*, and the gaff-topsail schooner *[Bill of Rights](http://www.americantallship.org/)*.  There will be music, food, arts and crafts, ship tours, historical reenactments and battle cruises. [Click here to learn more](http://www.tallshipsfestival.com/events.html).
