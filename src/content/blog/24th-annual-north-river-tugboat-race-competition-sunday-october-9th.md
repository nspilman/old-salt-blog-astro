![24thtugrace](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2016/10/24thtugrace.jpg)The 24th Annual North River Tugboat Race & Competition was originally scheduled for Labor Day, but [Tropical Storm Hermine interfered](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2016/09/24th-annual-great-north-river-tugboat-race-competition-postponed-hermine/). The rescheduled event, sponsored by the [Working Harbor Committee](http://workingharbor.com/) will be held on Sunday, October 9th from 9am-2pm at Pier 83, West 43rd Street and Hudson River Park, in New York City.  For those who wish to see the race up close and personal, there will be a Circle Line Spectator Boat. [Click here for more information and tickets](https://www.nycharities.org/Events/EventLevels.aspx?etid=9305).

The schedule of events:

*   10:00am Parade of Tugs from Pier 84 (w 44th st./Hudson river park) to the
*   start line @ Pier I at w 70th st. in Riverside Park South
*   10:30am Race Starts (runs from Pier I to Pier 84)
*   11:00am Nose-to-nose pushing contests & Line-toss competition
*   Noon Amateur line-toss & Spinach-eating contests
*   1:00pm Awards Ceremony
*   2:00pm Tugs depart
