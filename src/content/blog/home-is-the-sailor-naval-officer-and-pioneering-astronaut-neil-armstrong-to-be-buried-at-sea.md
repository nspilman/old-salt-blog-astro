[Neil Armstrong](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neil_Armstrong), the first man to set foot on the moon, best known for navigating the Sea of Tranquility, began his career as a Navy aviator.  Armstrong, who died on August 25, at the age of 82, will be buried at sea as per his wishes. NASA has scheduled a public memorial service to be held Sept. 13 at Washington National Cathedral.   No date has been set for the burial at sea which will be performed by the US Navy.
