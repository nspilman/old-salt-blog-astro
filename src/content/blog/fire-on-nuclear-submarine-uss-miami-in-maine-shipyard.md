Crew responded to a fire on the nuclear submarine, *[USS Miami](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Miami_\(SSN-755\)),*  at around 5:40 PM last night at the [Portsmouth Naval Shipyard](http://www.navsea.navy.mil/shipyards/portsmouth/default.aspx) in Kittery, Maine.  This morning, the Navy reports that the fire has been put put. Three shipyard firefighters, two civilian firefighters and two crew members are reported to have received minor injuries.  There were no weapons aboard the submarine and the reactor had been shut down for several months prior to the fire breaking out.  The *USS Miami* (SSN-755) is a United States Navy attack submarine of the Los Angeles class. She was commissioned in 1990. Groton, CT is her home port.
