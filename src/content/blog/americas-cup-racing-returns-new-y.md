![acjimmystatue](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/acjimmystatue.jpg)In early May, [America’s Cup racing will return to New York City](https://www.americascup.com/en/news/2091_Americas-Cup-racing-returns-to-New-York-2016-calendar-released.html) for the first time since 1920. The [Louis Vuitton America’s Cup World Series regatta](https://www.americascup.com/en/world-series-AC45.html), one of a series of warm-up races sailed in AC 45s, 45′ foiling catamarans,  prior to the main event, the [35th sailing of the America’s Cup Races](https://www.americascup.com/en/bermuda.html), will take place in Bermuda in 2017.  From the [AC website](https://www.americascup.com/en/news/2137_Americas-Cup-Racing-Returns-to-New-York-for-First-Time-in-96-Years.html): *On both Saturday, May 7 and Sunday, May 8, the epicenter of the race festivities will be Battery Park City and the Brookfield Place Waterfront Plaza. All races will finish just off the plaza, which will be home to one central event village. Thousands of spectators are expected to watch the race, either on land along the pier or from hundreds of spectator boats on the water near the race course.*
