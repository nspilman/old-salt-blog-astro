Patrick O’Brian is a well known name in nautical fiction.  Now another [Patrick O’Brien](http://www.patrickobrienstudio.com/), with a different spelling of his last name and an American, rather than an Englishman pretending to be Irish, is making a name for himself as a marine artist.  Here is a short video produced for the [National Maritime Historical Society](http://www.seahistory.org/) by [Rick Lopes](http://www.seahistory.org/about/our-crew/officers-trustees/richardo-r-lopes/) of XXLMedia.
