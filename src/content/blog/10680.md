[Endeavour Voyage 2010](http://www.anmm.gov.au/endeavour2010/)

The [Australian National Maritime Museum](http://www.anmm.gov.au/site/page.cfm) has an exciting season of sailing coming up on the replica of the *[HMB Endeavour](http://www.anmm.gov.au/site/page.cfm?u=1372).* This April, they are offering day sails in Sydney Harbor, to introduce visitors to 18th century sailing; overnight sails where the volunteer crew can sleep in hammocks, just like Cook’s crew; and five and six day voyages where the volunteers will help sail the ship while learning about navigation, meteorology and tall ship sailing.
