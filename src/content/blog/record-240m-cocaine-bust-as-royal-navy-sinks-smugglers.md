The Royal Navy in joint operations with the US Coast Guard off South America has seized a £240 million cocaine shipment – the biggest haul in Royal Navy history.   *[HMS Iron Duke](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Iron_Duke_\(F234\))*’s crew sank the 138ft drug boat with bursts of cannon fire after confiscating its cargo and arresting the smugglers.
