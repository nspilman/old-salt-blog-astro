A very interesting opportunity posted on the [Tall Ship Challenge Official Blog](http://tallshipschallenge.wordpress.com/2013/09/12/picton-castle-apprenticeship/): *Looking for more sea time and experience? [Picton Castle](http://www.picton-castle.com/) is looking for apprentices to join them in the Pacific. For the cost of room and board, you can join Leg 3 of the current voyage, sailing from Auckland, New Zealand to Nuku Hiva in the Marquesas, via Pitcairn Island and Mangareva, from October 25 this year to February 1 next year.*
