[![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/maritimeghost.jpg)](http://maritimeghostconference.com/)Tomorrow, September 15, a [Maritime Ghost Conference](http://maritimeghostconference.com/) will be held on the [Steam Ferry *Berkeley*](http://www.sdmaritime.org/steam-ferry-berkeley/) at the [Maritime Museum of San Diego](http://www.sdmaritime.org/). The conference is sponsored by the [San Diego Ghost and Paranormal Group](http://www.sandiegoghosthunters.com/spotlights/resources-sd-meet-up-group.html) and is also a fundraiser for the Maritime Museum. In addition to presentations by experts on various haunted ships, there will be investigations aboard the  windjammer [*Star of India*](http://www.sdmaritime.org/star-of-india/) and the [Steam Ferry *Berkeley*](http://www.sdmaritime.org/steam-ferry-berkeley/), both of which are said to be haunted.

If you can’t make the conference here is an episode of [Ghost Hunters](http://www.syfy.com/ghosthunters/), courtesy of You Tube, investigating reports of ghosts and the paranormal on the *Star of India* and the *Berkeley.*
