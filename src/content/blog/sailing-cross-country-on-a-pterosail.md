![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/pterosail.jpg)John MacTaggart is attempting to sail cross-country on a [Pterosail](http://www.pterosail.com/), a recumbent trike with a sail and solar panel.  He set off on July 1st in San Diego and is now in New Mexico on his way to Florida. He is now almost a third of the way on his over 3,000 mile trip.
