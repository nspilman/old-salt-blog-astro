A drone video of the 110-year-old battleship [USS *Texas*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Texas_\(BB-35\)) being refloated following repairs and refurbishment on the drydock at the Gulf Copper Shipyard in Galveston.  The *Texas* is the oldest remaining dreadnought battleship and only one of six surviving ships to have served in both World War I and World War II.
