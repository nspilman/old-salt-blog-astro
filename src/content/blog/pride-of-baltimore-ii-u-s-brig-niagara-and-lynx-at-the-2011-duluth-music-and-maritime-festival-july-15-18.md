![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2011/07/ship-photo.jpg)The  *[Pride of Baltimore II](http://www.pride2.org/index.php)*, the *[Brig Niagara](http://flagshipniagara.org/flagship_niagara/index.htm)*, and the [privateer Lynx](http://www.privateerlynx.com/) will on hand for the [2011 Duluth Music and Maritime Festival](http://visitduluth.com/maritimefestival/)  starting on Friday July 15 and running through Monday, July 18.  Music, food, and tall ships – it sounds like a great weekend.  The festival will also coincide with the [Sixth Annual Bayfront Reggae and World Music festival](http://www.bayfrontreggae.com/), the largest festival of it’s kind in Minnesota, to be held the same weekend at Bayfront Festival Park, July 16 – 17.

[Duluth Music and Maritime Festival](http://www.visitduluth.com/maritimefestival/)
