USS Constitution

The first exhibit in the $500 expansion of the Boston Museum of Fine Arts new Art of the Americas Wing in currently being installed.   The first new exhibit –  ship models.  An excellent choice.   The new wing opens in November.  For those in the Boston area who would rather not wait, the [USS Constitution Model Shipwright Guild’s Annual Model Ship Show – Models in the Spotlight](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/02/24/maritime-museum-round-up-models-spooks-anchors-and-a-new-museum/) runs through March 2oth at the [USS Constitution Museum](http://www.ussconstitutionmuseum.org/).
