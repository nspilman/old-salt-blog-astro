Philadelphia’s tall ship, the barquentine *[Gazela Primeiro](http://gazela.org/offerings/#)*, arrived in New York harbor yesterday, on her way to [PortSide New York](http://www.portsidenewyork.org/).  (See our previous post – [The Gazela at Portside with Vaudeville and Pirates – Oh My!](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/08/16/the-gazela-at-portside-with-vaudeville-and-pirates-oh-my/))   The classic ship will available for daytime tours starting today through Monday the 23rd. By night,  the ship will host Philadelphia’s [Cabaret Red Light’s “The Seven Deadly Seas”](http://www.cabaretredlight.com/sevenseas/home.html)   tonight through Sunday.   Here are a few photographs of the [Gazela](http://gazela.org/offerings/#) taken by Captain Richard Dorfman from the [schooner *Pioneer*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pioneer_\(schooner\)).  Click on the thumbnails to view larger images.
