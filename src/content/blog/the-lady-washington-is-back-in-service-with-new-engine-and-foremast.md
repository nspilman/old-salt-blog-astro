![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/ladywashington.jpg)There is a certain irony that a sailing ship would have to be taken out of service because its engine did not meet current emission standards, but such was the case with the *[Lady Washington](http://www.historicalseaport.org/web/lady-washington.html),* the Official Ship of the State of Washington. She ran afoul of  tough new California pollution regulations.  She is now, however, back in service, with a new cleaner engine as well as a new foremast.  The *Lady Washington* should be rejoining the *[Hawaiian Chieftain](http://www.historicalseaport.org/web/hawaiian-chieftain.html)* on April 10th.
