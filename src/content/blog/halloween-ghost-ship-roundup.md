On this eve of the eve of All Hallows Eve, it seems worthwhile to list a few of the “ghost” ships open to the public on Halloween.

[Hornet Ship of Spirits](http://usshornet.stores.yahoo.net/hoshofspghin.html) – This evening , on the historic aircraft carrier *[USS Hornet](http://www.uss-hornet.org/)* in  Alameda CA, there will be a presentation the real stories of the ghosts on the *USS Hornet*, followed on Saturday by a *USS Hornet* [Monster Bash](http://www.uss-hornet.org/calendar/halloween/index.shtml).

[The Haunted Ship at the USS Salem](http://www.hauntedship.com/main.htm) – Spooky tours and events through Sunday on the heavy cruiser *[USS Salem](http://www.uss-salem.org/)* in Quincy, MA.

[Captain Fishsticker’s Ship of Horrors](http://www.zvents.com/philadelphia-pa/events/show/146749585-captain-fishstickers-ship-of-horrors) – [Revival Burlesque](http://www.facebook.com/pages/Revival-Burlesque/102488448499) is performing “*Captain Fishsticker’s Ship of Horrors”* tonight through Sunday on the lovely *[Barkentine Gazela](http://gazela.org/ships/gazela/gazela.htm)* in Philadelphia, PA.  
  
[Fifth annual Halloween Haunted Ship aboard the USS Turner Joy](http://www.kitsapsun.com/news/2010/oct/24/halloween-events/) – Festivities in Bremerton, WA on the Vietnam War era destroyer, *[Turner Joy](http://www.ussturnerjoy.org/)*.

[U.S. Coast Guard Cutter Mobile Bay Ghost Ship](http://www.wfrv.com/onlyon5/calendar/102464249.html) – In Sturgeon Bay, WI, kids and adults will be ushered through various haunted rooms throughout the  *USCGC [Mobile Bay](http://www.uscg.mil/d9/cgcmobilebay/)*.  Treats will be available for the kids.

[Ghost Ship: Battleship North Carolina](http://www.battleshipnc.com/Events/EventCalendar/tabid/61/ModuleID/381/ItemID/26/mctl/EventDetails/Default.aspx) – Spooky fun on the haunted *[USS North Carolina](http://www.battleshipnc.com/)* in Wilmington, NC.

[Ghostly Sea Shanty Boat Trip](http://www.discoverireland.com/us/ireland-things-to-see-and-do/whats-on/listings/gss/product/?fid=NITB_19176) – On the other side of the pond in Belfast harbor, the [Ghostly Sea Shanty Ship](http://www.laganboatcompany.com/) will sail the harbour today and tommorrow with its scary complement of passengers dressed as Ghosts, Ghouls & Witches!!

[Halloween Ghost Ship Ball on HMS President](http://www.residentadvisor.net/event.aspx?190389) – A fancy dress ball on the *[HMS President](http://maps.google.com/maps/place?um=1&ie=UTF-8&q=HMS+President&fb=1&gl=us&hq=HMS+President&cid=3586429836975287787)* (1918) moored in Victoria Embankment, Blackfriars, London for a voyage of horrific & freighting fun for all.

[William A. Irvin Haunted Ship](http://duluthhauntedship.com/) – Tours in Duluth, Minnesota of the haunted laker *[William A. Irvin](http://duluthhauntedship.com/)*

[No Zombies on the LST](http://www.mlive.com/news/muskegon/index.ssf/2010/10/muskegon_officials_sink_lst_zo.html) – Sadly the LST Zombie Ship in Muskegon, MI has been sunk this year by a lack of city permits.   Who knew that the un-dead could be vanquished by bureaucracy.
