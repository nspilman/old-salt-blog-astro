![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2012/03/lehman.jpg)The Staten Island ferry *Herbert H. Lehman* was decommissioned in 2007 and [put up for sale last year on EBay for $500,000](http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2011/02/former_staten_island_ferry_her.html).  The 297′ foot Kennedy class ferry boat apparently sprang a leak on Friday at her berth in Newburg, N.Y. and is now listing and partially flooded, sitting on the Hudson River bottom.
