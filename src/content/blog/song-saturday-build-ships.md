Singer/songwriter [Tom Munch](http://tommunch.com/Tom_Munch/Home.html) plays his original song about the last great historic wooden schooners of Maine in the early 20th century. The photos are historic shots of the [*Wyoming*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wyoming_\(schooner\)) and other ships built at Percy and Small shipyard in Bath, Maine and elsewhere.
