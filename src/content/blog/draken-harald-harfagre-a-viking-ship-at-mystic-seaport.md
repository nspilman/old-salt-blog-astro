Following its epic voyage across the Atlantic, with stops in visiting Iceland and Greenland, the [*Draken Harald Hårfagre*](http://www.drakenexpeditionamerica.com/the-ship/), toured the Great Lakes, traveled down the Erie Canal, stopped by New York City and then wintered at [Mystic Seaport Museum](https://www.mysticseaport.org/). This summer, the largest Viking long ship built in modern times has been on display at the Seaport museum.  A US [East Coast tour in 2018](http://www.drakenexpeditionamerica.com/expedition-america-east-coast-tour-2018/) is currently being planned.
