For nearly 700 years, fishermen have been trawling for shrimp from horseback in the small town of Oostduinkerke, on the Belgian coast. Whether the tradition is [dying](https://www.atlasobscura.com/articles/belgium-fishing-shrimp-on-horses) or [enduring](https://theculturetrip.com/europe/belgium/articles/the-belgian-tradition-of-shrimp-fishing-on-horseback/) may be the subject of debate.  It is, however, both singular and fascinating.
