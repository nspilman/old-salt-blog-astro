As we posted yesterday, over last weekend, the [Maritime Museum of San Diego celebrated the 155th birthday](https://sdmaritime.org/latest-news/celebrating-star-of-indias-155-years/) of the [*Star of India*](https://sdmaritime.org/visit/the-ships/star-of-india/) by taking her for a sail. Here is nicely done, roughly ten-minute, video by [Barrett Canfield](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC_dxKjaYuVCPlFK-ZVK6nlQ) of Sunday’s sail on the grand old ship.  The museum’s other ships [*Californian*](https://sdmaritime.org/visit/the-ships/californian/), [*San Salvador*](https://sdmaritime.org/visit/the-ships/san-salvador/), and [America](https://sdmaritime.org/visit/the-ships/america/) can be seen sailing along with with the *Star of India*.
