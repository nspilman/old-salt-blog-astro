In 1780, the French frigate, *[l’Hermione](http://www.hermione2015.com/index.html)* carried the Marquis de Lafayette to America with the news of French support for the American revolution. In the next month or so, the newly completed replica frigate [*l’Hermione* will recreate that historic voyage.](http://www.hermione2015.com/history.html#voyage_2015) Here is a video of *l’Hermione* on sea trials last fall.
