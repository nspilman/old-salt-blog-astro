An interesting Kickstarter fundraiser to complete the documentary [“Twice Forgotten: Heroes of the R12 Submarine](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/184288804/twice-forgotten-story-of-the-wwii-submarine-r-12),” about the [WWII submarine USS R-12](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_R-12_\(SS-89\)), which sank off the coast of Key West, Florida on June 12, 1943 with the loss of 40 American sailors and 2 Brazilian officers.  The wreckage of the submarine was located in 2011 in 600 feet of water.
