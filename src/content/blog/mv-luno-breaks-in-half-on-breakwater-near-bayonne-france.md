The [MV Luno](http://www.vesselfinder.com/vessels/LUNO-IMO-9072329-MMSI-224544000), a 4,600 DWT Spanish general cargo ship, lost power in rough seas and high winds  and and was blown onto a breakwater south-west of French port city Bayonne.  In winds gusting up to blowing up to 110 Km/hr (68 mph), the ship broke in half and sank. The crew was rescued by helicopter. One crew member was reported injured.   Diesel oil has been spotted in the water around the sections of the ship.  Local marine authorities are reported to be activating their anti-maritime pollution plan. Thanks to Irwin Bryan for passing along the news.
