![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/Sarah-Breton.jpg)Sarah Breton, 45, from Essex, has been appointed as Captain of the 1,200 P&O cruise ship *Artemis*. As such she is the first female captain in P&O’s 173 year history and the first female captain of a cruise ship in Britain.
