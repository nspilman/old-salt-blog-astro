![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2010/06/scottcardiff1.jpg)Yesterday was the 100th anniversary of [Captain Scott](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Falcon_Scott)‘s departure from Cardiff on his ill-fated expedition to reach the South Pole.    The tall ship [](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stavros_S_Niarchos_\(ship\))*[Stavros S Niarchos](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stavros_S_Niarchos_\(ship\))* sailed across Cardiff Bay, reenacting the departure of Scott’s ship, the *Terra Nova*.   The Royal Navy’s [*HMS Scott*](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Scott_\(H131\)) also participated in the ceremony.  Click on the link below for a video.   See also our previous post: [The Saga of Robert Falcon Scott – Complex and Controversial](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2010/06/06/the-saga-of-robert-falcon-scott-complex-and-controversial/)
