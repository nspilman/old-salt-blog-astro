The [32nd Annual Sea Music Festival](http://www.mysticseaport.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=home.viewPage&page_id=C9BE0FCF-D86E-382C-FA65352B6393646D) at [Mystic Seaport](http://www.mysticseaport.org/) starts this Thursday night, June 9th, and runs through Sunday, June 12th.    More than 5,000 people gather each year to hear [Mystic Seaport’s Chantey Staff](http://www.mysticseaport.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=home.viewPage&page_id=BFEF20C9-1E4F-379B-6035431F001BA25F) along with a solid core of performers carry on classic musical traditions of the golden age of sail.  The festival features music from maritime cultures around the world, including the United Kingdom, Italy, Portugal, Australia, Iceland, Poland, Netherlands, France, Canada and Africa, as well as native peoples within the United States.  In addition to the performances, the [32nd Annual Music of the Sea Symposium](http://www.mysticseaport.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=home.viewPage&page_id=47E8BDB0-65B8-D398-7B968471E18B4FAD) will also take place on Saturday and Sunday.
