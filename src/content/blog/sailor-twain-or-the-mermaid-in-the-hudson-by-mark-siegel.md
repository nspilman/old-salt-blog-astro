I have been a fan of Sailor Twain for some time. [*Sailor Twain, or The Mermaid in the Hudson*](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1596436360/) is a webcomic by Mark Siegel. He has been posting pages online every Monday, Wednesday and Friday, the tale unfolding in the tradition of a 19th century novel. For those of us who haven’t always kept up, the graphic novel is being published in its entirety on October 2, 2012.
