![bataan](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2016/05/bataan.jpg)Yesterday thousands lined the shores of the Hudson to watch the [Fleet Week Parade of Ships](http://www.fleetweeknewyork.com/fleetweeknewyork/events.html). Unfortunately, this year, I couldn’t be there. Here, courtesy of the US Navy, is a unique view of the festivities from the flight deck of one of the main attractions, [USS *Bataan*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Bataan_\(LHD-5\)). USS *Bataan* (LHD-5) is a Wasp-class amphibious assault ship commissioned in 1997. The ship carried more than 500 Marines and Sailors with the 24th Marine Expeditionary Unit who will participate in this year’s Fleet Week.
