![](https://old-salt-blog-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/2011/10/507520081_o.jpg)Last March we [posted that the U. S. Coast Guard Cutter *Acushne**t*](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/2011/03/03/u-s-coast-guard-cutter-acushnet-queen-of-the-fleet-for-sale/),  the oldest commissioned Coast Guard cutter, was being sold in an online auction.   The ship was reportedly sold to an unidentified buyer.  The ship has now been put up for auction on EBay by “[masterbushpilot0](http://myworld.ebay.com/ebaymotors/masterbushpilot0/?_trksid=p4340.l2559).”   The “Buy It Now” price has been set at $10,000,000. The current highest bid is $27,100 and the reserve price has not been met.  The auction ends in four days.
