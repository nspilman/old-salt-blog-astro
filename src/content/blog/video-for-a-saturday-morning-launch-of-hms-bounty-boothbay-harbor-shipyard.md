A relaxing video for a Saturday morning. [HMS *Bounty*](http://www.tallshipbounty.org/) slides gracefully back into the water at Boothbay HArbor Shipyard last Thursday.  Thanks to [Tom Russell](http://www.linkedin.com/profile/view?id=13370427&authType=name&authToken=etG3&goback=%2Eamf_2041742_13370427&trk=anetppl_profil) on the Linked-in [Traditional Sail Professionals](http://www.linkedin.com/groups?gid=2041742&trk=myg_ugrp_ovr) group for pointing it out.  In other news, HMS *Bounty* will be [returning to St. Petersburg for one weekend, November 10-11.](http://www.tampabay.com/features/events/article1257392.ece)  Thanks to Ann Brown for the heads up.
