A relaxing video for a Saturday afternoon. I’ve long been a fan of [Fred LeBlanc’s photography](http://fredleblancphotography.com/), particularly his photos of schooners. Here is a video of LeBlanc’s photos of scenes from [Maine Windjammers fleet](http://www.sailmainecoast.com/). LeBlanc hosts photo sailing adventure aboard the [schooner *Heritage*](http://www.schoonerheritage.com/). His next is scheduled for June 10 – 14, 2017, sailing from Rockland, Maine.
