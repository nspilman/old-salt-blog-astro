![](http://www.oldsaltblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/missingyacht.jpg)Paul Janse van Rensburg’s sailboat, *Tafadzwa*, was found drifting near the Chatham Islands in the Pacific east of New Zealand.  A search of the boat found van Rensburg’s dog  alive and the boat’s life raft still aboard.   No sign was found of  van Rensburg who has been missing for 16 days.
